Fumitremorgin b, also known as lanosulin, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as beta carbolines. Beta carbolines are compounds containing a 9H-pyrido[3, 4-b]indole moiety. Fumitremorgin b exists as a solid and is considered to be practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral. Within the cell, fumitremorgin b is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Fumitremorgin b is a potentially toxic compound.
