C. i. acid green 50, also known as FOOD green S or it, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as sulfonated stilbenes. These are stilbenes that carry a sulfone group at one or more positions of either benzene rings. C. i. acid green 50 is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, c. i. acid green 50 is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
