A potassium channel opener, it is isolated from Mallotus philippensis. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an apoptosis inducer, a metabolite, a K-ATP channel agonist, an antihypertensive agent and an anti-allergic agent. It is an enone, a chromenol, a benzenetriol, a methyl ketone and an aromatic ketone.
