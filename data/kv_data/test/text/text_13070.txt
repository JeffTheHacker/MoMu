3-(4-Hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl)-1-(2, 4, 6-trihydroxyphenyl)prop-2-en-1-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 2'-hydroxychalcones. These are organic compounds containing chalcone skeleton that carries a hydroxyl group at the 2'-position. 3-(4-Hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl)-1-(2, 4, 6-trihydroxyphenyl)prop-2-en-1-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
