Used as a fungicide. it is a member of the class of N-alkylpiperazines in which the two amino groups of piperazine are replaced by 1-formamido-2,2,2-trichloroethyl groups. A fungicide active against a range of diseases including powdery mildew, scab and rust. It has a role as an EC 1.14.13.70 (sterol 14alpha-demethylase) inhibitor, an allergen and an antifungal agrochemical. It is a N-alkylpiperazine, a member of formamides, an organochlorine compound and an amide fungicide.
