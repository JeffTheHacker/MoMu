Trichlopyr is a monocarboxylic acid that is (pyridin-2-yloxy)acetic acid substituted by chloro groups at positions 3, 5 and 6. It is an agrochemical used as a herbicide. It has a role as an environmental contaminant, a xenobiotic, a herbicide and an agrochemical. It is an aromatic ether, a chloropyridine and a monocarboxylic acid.
