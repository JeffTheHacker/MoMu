N-(4-Aminobutyl)-3-(4-hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl)prop-2-enimidic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxycinnamic acids and derivatives. Hydroxycinnamic acids and derivatives are compounds containing an cinnamic acid (or a derivative thereof) where the benzene ring is hydroxylated. N-(4-Aminobutyl)-3-(4-hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl)prop-2-enimidic acid is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
