Hexagonal leaflets (from alcohols) or prisms (from acetic acid) or white powder. (NTP, 1992) it is a monohydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid with a alpha-hydroxy substituent at position 3. It is a bile acid obtained from chenodeoxycholic acid by bacterial action. It has a role as a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a bile acid, a monohydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid and a C24-steroid. It is a conjugate acid of a lithocholate.
