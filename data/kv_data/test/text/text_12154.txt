1, 2-Butanedithiol, also known as 1, 2-dimercaptobutane or fema 3528, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as alkylthiols. These are organic compounds containing the thiol functional group linked to an alkyl chain. 1, 2-Butanedithiol is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, 1, 2-butanedithiol is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
