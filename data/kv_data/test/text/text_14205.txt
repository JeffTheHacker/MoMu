N-acetyl-L-alpha-phenylglycine is an N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid that is L-alpha-phenylglycine in which a hydrogen attached to the nitrogen has been replaced by an acetyl group. It is a N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid, a glycine derivative and a non-proteinogenic amino acid derivative. It derives from a L-alpha-phenylglycine. It is a conjugate acid of a N-acetyl-L-alpha-phenylglycinate.
