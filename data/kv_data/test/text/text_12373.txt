it is a member of the class of benzoxazine that is 6-hydroxy-1,4-benzoxazin-3-one in which the hydrogen at position 4 is replaced by a (1R)-1-hydroxy-2-{[1-(4-methoxyphenyl)-2-methylpropan-2-yl]amino}ethyl group. Used (as its hydrochloride salt) for long-term treatment of airflow obstruction in patients with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease including chronic bronchitis and/or emphysema. It has a role as a beta-adrenergic agonist and a bronchodilator agent. It is a benzoxazine, a member of phenols, an aromatic ether, a secondary alcohol and a secondary amino compound. It is a conjugate base of an it(1+).
