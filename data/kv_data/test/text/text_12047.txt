Dihydro-2-methyl-3(2H)-furanthione, also known as 4, 5-dihydro-3-mercapto-2-methylfuran, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as dihydrofurans. Dihydrofurans are compounds containing a dihydrofuran moiety, which is a furan derivative with only one double bond. Dihydro-2-methyl-3(2H)-furanthione is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
