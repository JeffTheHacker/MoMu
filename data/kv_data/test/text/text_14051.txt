trans-3-Coumarate, also known as 3-hydroxycinnamate or m-coumarate, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxycinnamic acids. Hydroxycinnamic acids are compounds containing an cinnamic acid where the benzene ring is hydroxylated. trans-3-Coumarate is slightly soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
