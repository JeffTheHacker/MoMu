2-(3, 4, 5-Trihydroxyphenyl)acetic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as pyrogallols and derivatives. Pyrogallols and derivatives are compounds containing a 1, 2, 3-trihydroxybenzene moiety. 2-(3, 4, 5-Trihydroxyphenyl)acetic acid is slightly soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
