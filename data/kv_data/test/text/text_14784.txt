(2E)-3-(2, 4-Dihydroxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid, also known as (e)-24-dihydroxycinnamic acid or 2, 4-dihydroxy-trans cinnamate, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxycinnamic acids. Hydroxycinnamic acids are compounds containing an cinnamic acid where the benzene ring is hydroxylated (2E)-3-(2, 4-Dihydroxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid is slightly soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
