(e)-2-Octene belongs to the class of organic compounds known as unsaturated aliphatic hydrocarbons. These are aliphatic Hydrocarbons that contains one or more unsaturated carbon atoms. These compounds contain one or more double or triple bonds (e)-2-Octene is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule (e)-2-Octene has been detected in multiple biofluids, such as feces and saliva. Outside of the human body, (e)-2-octene can be found in mango. This makes (e)-2-octene a potential biomarker for the consumption of this food product.
