These are organic compounds containing a psoralen moiety, which consists of a furan fused to a chromenone to for 7H-furo[3, 2-g]chromen-7-one. 3-Hydroxy-2-(2-hydroxypropan-2-yl)-2H, 3H, 7H-furo[3, 2-g]chromen-7-one is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
