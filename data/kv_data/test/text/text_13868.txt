Selective, peripherally acting dopamine D2 receptor agonist. Modulates noradrenalin release and sympathetic activation. Displays antihypertensive properties in vivo. It has a role as an antihypertensive agent, a dopamine agonist and a platelet aggregation inhibitor. It is an indolecarboxylic acid, a tetrahydropyridine and a tertiary amino compound.
