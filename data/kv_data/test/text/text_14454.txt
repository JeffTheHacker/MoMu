Thus, 2, 6-dimethyl-6-hepten-1-ol is considered to be a fatty alcohol lipid molecule. 2, 6-Dimethyl-6-hepten-1-ol is slightly soluble (in water) and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). 2, 6-Dimethyl-6-hepten-1-ol has been primarily detected in urine. Within the cell, 2, 6-dimethyl-6-hepten-1-ol is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2, 6-Dimethyl-6-hepten-1-ol has a melon taste.
