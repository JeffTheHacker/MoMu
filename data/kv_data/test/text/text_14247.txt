It is a potent loop diuretic used for the treatment of hypertension and edema in patients with congestive heart failure. It has a role as a loop diuretic and an antihypertensive agent. It is a N-sulfonylurea, an aminopyridine and a secondary amino compound. It derives from a 4-aminopyridine. it is an anilinopyridine sulfonylurea belonging to the class of loop diuretics. it has a prolonged duration of action compared to other loop diuretics, is extensively protein bound in plasma and has a relatively long half-life.
