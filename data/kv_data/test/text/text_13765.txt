3, 3'-Thiobispropanoic acid, also known as it or 4-thiaheptanedioic acid, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as dicarboxylic acids and derivatives. These are organic compounds containing exactly two carboxylic acid groups. 3, 3'-Thiobispropanoic acid exists as a solid, slightly soluble (in water), and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 3, 3'-thiobispropanoic acid is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
