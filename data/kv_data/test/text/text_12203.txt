Diphenyloxide-4,4&#39;-disulfonyl hydrazide appears as a colorless, crystalline solid with a geranium-like odor. Insoluble in water and denser than water. Contact may irritate skin, eyes and mucous membranes. Moderately toxic by ingestion, inhalation and skin absorption. Readily ignited by sparks or flames and burns intensely and persistently.
