It is the primary (and long-lived) metabolite of ibogaine, the psychoactive indole alkaloid found in the African rainforest shrub Tabernanthe iboga. It has a role as a psychotropic drug, a serotonin uptake inhibitor, a NMDA receptor antagonist and a kappa-opioid receptor agonist.
