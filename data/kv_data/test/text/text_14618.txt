It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an estrogen antagonist, an estrogen receptor modulator and a bone density conservation agent. It derives from a hydride of a stilbene. it is a nonsteroidal triphenylethylene antiestrogen. Chemically related to tamoxifen, it is a selective estrogen receptor modulator (SERM).
