Colorless in presence of large amounts of alkali. Tasteless. (NTP, 1992) it is a member of phenols. It derives from a 2-benzofuran-1(3H)-one. it is an organic compound used as a laboratory reagent and pH indicator. it exerts laxative effects by stimulating the intestinal mucosa and constricting smooth muscles. However, it is no longer used as a laxative due to the suspected carcinogenicity of this compound.
