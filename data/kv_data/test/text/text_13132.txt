Isolated from the Mediterranean shallow-water sponge, Topsentia genetrix. It is a potent inhibitor of MRSA pyruvate kinase and exhibits antibacterial properties. It has a role as an antibacterial agent, a marine metabolite and an EC 2.7.1.40 (pyruvate kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of imidazoles, a bromoindole, an aromatic ketone and an indole alkaloid.
