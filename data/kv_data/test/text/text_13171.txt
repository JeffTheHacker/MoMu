it is an anthocyanin cation consisting of pelargonidin having a rutinosyl [6-deoxy-alpha-L-mannosyl-(1->6)-beta-D-glucosyl] residue attached at the 3-hydroxy position and a beta-D-glucosyl residue at the 5-hydroxy position. It is an anthocyanin cation, a rutinoside, a beta-D-glucoside and a disaccharide derivative. It is a conjugate acid of a pelargonidin 3-O-rutinoside 5-O-beta-D-glucoside betaine.
