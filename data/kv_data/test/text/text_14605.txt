3-(4-Hydroxyphenyl)-3, 4-dihydro-2H-1-benzopyran-7, 8-diol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as isoflavanols. These are polycyclic compounds containing a hydroxylated isoflavan skeleton. 3-(4-Hydroxyphenyl)-3, 4-dihydro-2H-1-benzopyran-7, 8-diol is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
