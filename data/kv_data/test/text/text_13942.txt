These are organic compounds containing a ketone that is conjugated to a cyclic moiety. 2-Hydroxy-3, 4, 5-trimethyl-2-cyclopenten-1-one is soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2-hydroxy-3, 4, 5-trimethyl-2-cyclopenten-1-one is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Outside of the human body, 2-hydroxy-3, 4, 5-trimethyl-2-cyclopenten-1-one can be found in coffee and coffee products. This makes 2-hydroxy-3, 4, 5-trimethyl-2-cyclopenten-1-one a potential biomarker for the consumption of this food product.
