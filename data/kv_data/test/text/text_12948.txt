5, 7-Dimethoxy-3-phenyl-3, 4-dihydro-2H-1-benzopyran-4-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 7-o-methylated isoflavonoids. These are isoflavonoids with methoxy groups attached to the C7 atom of the isoflavonoid backbone. Isoflavonoids are natural products derived from 3-phenylchromen-4-one. 5, 7-Dimethoxy-3-phenyl-3, 4-dihydro-2H-1-benzopyran-4-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
