2, 3, 4, 5-Tetrahydroxypentanoic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as sugar acids and derivatives. Sugar acids and derivatives are compounds containing a saccharide unit which bears a carboxylic acid group. 2, 3, 4, 5-Tetrahydroxypentanoic acid is soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). 2, 3, 4, 5-Tetrahydroxypentanoic acid has been primarily detected in urine.
