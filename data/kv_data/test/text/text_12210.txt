(E)-it is a member of the class of cinnamaldehydes that is cinnamaldehyde substituted by a hydroxy group at position 4 and methoxy groups at positions 3 and 5. It has a role as an antifungal agent and a plant metabolite. It is a member of cinnamaldehydes, a dimethoxybenzene and a member of phenols. It derives from an (E)-cinnamaldehyde.
