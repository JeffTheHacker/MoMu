1-(2-Furanyl)-1-pentanone, also known as it or 2-valeroylfuran, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as aryl alkyl ketones. These are ketones have the generic structure RC(=O)R', where R = aryl group and R'=alkyl group. 1-(2-Furanyl)-1-pentanone is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, 1-(2-furanyl)-1-pentanone is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 1-(2-Furanyl)-1-pentanone has a sweet and caramel taste.
