C.i. it is a dark green powder. A dye derived from triphenylmethane. Rosanilin is a hydrochloride that is the monohydrochloride of 4-[(4-aminophenyl)(4-iminocyclohexa-2,5-dien-1-ylidene)methyl]-2-methylaniline. One of the major constituents of Basic it, together with pararosanilin, magenta II and new it. It has a role as a fluorochrome, a histological dye and a carcinogenic agent. It contains a rosanilin(1+).
