3-(4-Hydroxy-3, 5-dimethoxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid, also known as sinapic acid or 3, 5-dimethoxy-4-hydroxycinnamic acid, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxycinnamic acids. Hydroxycinnamic acids are compounds containing an cinnamic acid where the benzene ring is hydroxylated. 3-(4-Hydroxy-3, 5-dimethoxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
