N-{2-[4-methoxy-3-(2-phenylethoxy)phenyl]ethyl}-N-propylpropan-1-amine hydrochloride is an aromatic ether that is (2-phenoxyethyl)benzene which carries a 2-(dipropylamino)ethyl group at position 3 and a methoxy group at position 6. It is a potent and selective sigma1 receptor antagonist. It has a role as an antipsychotic agent and a receptor modulator. It is an aromatic ether, a member of methoxybenzenes, a tertiary amino compound and a hydrochloride.
