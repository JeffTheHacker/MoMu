Ethane-1, 1-dithiol, also known as 1, 1-it or bisedt, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as alkylthiols. These are organic compounds containing the thiol functional group linked to an alkyl chain. Ethane-1, 1-dithiol is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, ethane-1, 1-dithiol is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Ethane-1, 1-dithiol has a meaty and roasted taste.
