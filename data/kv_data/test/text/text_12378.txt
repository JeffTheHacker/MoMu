6-O-Phosphonato-D-glucono-1, 5-lactone(2-), also known as 6-PGDL or 6-phosphonoglucono-delta-lactone, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hexose phosphates. These are carbohydrate derivatives containing a hexose substituted by one or more phosphate groups. 6-O-Phosphonato-D-glucono-1, 5-lactone(2-) is soluble (in water) and a moderately acidic compound (based on its pKa).
