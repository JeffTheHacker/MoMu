1, 4-Bis-(1, 1-dimethylethyl)-benzene, also known as para-di-tert-butylbenzene, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as phenylpropanes. These are organic compounds containing a phenylpropane moiety. 1, 4-Bis-(1, 1-dimethylethyl)-benzene is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. 1, 4-Bis-(1, 1-dimethylethyl)-benzene has been primarily detected in feces.
