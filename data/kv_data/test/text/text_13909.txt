Kaempferol 3,7-di-O-beta-D-glucoside is a glycosyloxyflavone that is kaempferol having two beta-D-glucosyl residues attached at positions O-3 and O-7. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavone, a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a polyphenol and a kaempferol O-glucoside.
