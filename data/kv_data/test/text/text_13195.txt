9-(tetrahydrofuryl)adenine is a nucleoside analogue that is adenine in which the nitrogen at position 9 has been substituted by a tetrahydrofuran-2-yl group. It is an adenylate cyclase inhibitor. It has a role as an EC 4.6.1.1 (adenylate cyclase) inhibitor. It is a nucleoside analogue and a member of oxolanes. It derives from an adenine.
