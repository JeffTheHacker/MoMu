4-Methoxybenzene-1, 2-diol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as methoxyphenols. Methoxyphenols are compounds containing a methoxy group attached to the benzene ring of a phenol moiety. 4-Methoxybenzene-1, 2-diol is soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
