N-cyclopropyl-4-[4-(3,4-dichlorophenyl)-2-(1-methylpiperidin-4-yl)-1-propylimidazol-5-yl]pyrimidin-2-amine is a member of the class of piperidinylimidazoles that is 1-methyl-4-(1-propylimidazol-2-yl)piperidine carrying additional 3,4-dichlorophenyl and 2-(cyclopropylamino)pyrimidin-4-yl substituents at positions 4 and 5 respectively. It has a role as a c-Jun N-terminal kinase inhibitor. It is an aminopyrimidine, a piperidinylimidazole, a dichlorobenzene, a member of cyclopropanes and a tertiary amino compound.
