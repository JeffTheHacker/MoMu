Used as a fungicide. 1-[2-(allyloxy)-2-(2,4-dichlorophenyl)ethyl]imidazole is a member of the class of imidazoles in which the hydrogen at position 1 is replaced by a 2-(allyloxy)-2-(2,4-dichlorophenyl)ethyl group. It is a member of imidazoles, an ether and a dichlorobenzene.
