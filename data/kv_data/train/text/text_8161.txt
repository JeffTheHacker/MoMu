7-Hydroxy-3-phenyl-3, 4-dihydro-2H-1-benzopyran-4-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as isoflavanones. These are polycyclic compounds containing an isoflavan skeleton which bears a ketone at position C4. 7-Hydroxy-3-phenyl-3, 4-dihydro-2H-1-benzopyran-4-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
