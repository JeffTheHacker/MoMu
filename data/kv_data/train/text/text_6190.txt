It is a component of Gentiana scabra root extract, which is used as an internal remedy in traditional Korean medicine for the treatment of diabetes mellitus. It is a beta-D-glucoside, an acetate ester, a member of phenols, a delta-lactone, a pyranopyranone and a secoiridoid glycoside.
