4',5,7-trihydroxy-3'-methoxyflavone is the 3'-O-methyl derivative of luteolin. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an antioxidant and a metabolite. It is a trihydroxyflavone and a monomethoxyflavone. It derives from a luteolin. It is a conjugate acid of a 4',5-dihydroxy-3'-methoxyflavon-7-olate(1-).
