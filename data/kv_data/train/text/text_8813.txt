3-(3-Hydroxy-4-methoxyphenyl)-1-(2, 4, 6-trihydroxyphenyl)propan-1-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 2'-hydroxy-dihydrochalcones. These are organic compounds containing dihydrochalcone skeleton that carries a hydroxyl group at the 2'-position. 3-(3-Hydroxy-4-methoxyphenyl)-1-(2, 4, 6-trihydroxyphenyl)propan-1-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
