9-fluoro-2alpha-methylpregn-4-ene-3,11,20-trione is a fluorinated steroid that is pregn-4-ene substituted by a fluoro group at position 2, a methyl group at position 2 and oxo groups at positions 3, 11 and 20. It is a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, an 11-oxo steroid, a 20-oxo steroid and a fluorinated steroid. It derives from a progesterone. It derives from a hydride of a pregnane.
