9-Hydroxy-8-(2-hydroxypropan-2-yl)-2H, 8H, 9H-furo[2, 3-H]chromen-2-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as angular furanocoumarins. These are furanocoumarins, with a structure characterized by a furan ring angularly fused to a coumarin. 9-Hydroxy-8-(2-hydroxypropan-2-yl)-2H, 8H, 9H-furo[2, 3-H]chromen-2-one is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
