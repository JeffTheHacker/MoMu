A fungicide first marketed in 1971 and used in the cultivation of fruit and vegetables, as well as in wood preservatives, it is no longer approved for use in the European Union. It has a role as a genotoxin and an antifungal agrochemical. It is an organofluorine compound, an organochlorine compound, a member of sulfamides and a phenylsulfamide fungicide. It derives from a sulfamide.
