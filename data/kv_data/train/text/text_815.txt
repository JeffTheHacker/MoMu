2, 3-Dihydroxy-4-methoxybenzoic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as p-methoxybenzoic acids and derivatives. These are benzoic acids in which the hydrogen atom at position 4 of the benzene ring is replaced by a methoxy group. 2, 3-Dihydroxy-4-methoxybenzoic acid is slightly soluble (in water) and a moderately acidic compound (based on its pKa).
