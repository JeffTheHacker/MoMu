BGT226 is the maleate salt of 8-(6-methoxypyridin-3-yl)-3-methyl-1-[4-(piperazin-1-yl)-3-(trifluoromethyl)phenyl]-1,3-dihydro-2H-imidazo[4,5-c]quinolin-2-one. A dual PI3K/mTOR inhibitor. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, a mTOR inhibitor and an EC 2.7.1.137 (phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase) inhibitor. It contains a BGT226(1+).
