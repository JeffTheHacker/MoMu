A lipid-soluble iron-chelator used for treatment of thalassaemia. It has a role as an iron chelator and a protective agent. it is an orally bioavailable bidentate ligand with iron chelating activity. it binds to iron in a 3:1 (ligand:iron) molar ratio. By binding to iron, it is able to remove excess iron from the body.
