2',3'-cyclic CMP is a 2',3'-cyclic pyrimidine nucleotide in which cytidine is the parent nucleoside. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite, a mouse metabolite and a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a 2',3'-cyclic CMP(1-). it is a cyclic cytidine.it is formed during digestion of ribonucleic acid by ribonuclease.
