N'-[(4-oxo-4H-chromen-3-yl)methylene]nicotinohydrazide is a carbohydrazide that is nicotinohydrazide in which the terminal nitrogen has undergone formal condensation with the aldehyde group of 4-oxo-4H-chromene-3-carbaldehyde. It is an inhibitor of it (Signal Transducer and Activator of Transcription 5) protein. It has a role as a signal transducer and activator of transcription 5 protein inhibitor. It is a carbohydrazide, a member of chromones and a member of pyridines.
