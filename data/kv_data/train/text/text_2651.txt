A cholecystokinin antagonist used for treatment of gastrointestinal disorders. It has a role as a cholecystokinin antagonist, a gastrointestinal drug, an antineoplastic agent and an apoptosis inducer. It is a 1,4-benzodiazepinone and an indolecarboxamide.
