A histamine H1 receptor antagonist, it is used (commonly as its maleate salt) for the symptomatic relief of allergic conditions, including rhinitis and conjunctivitis. It has a role as a H1-receptor antagonist and an anti-allergic agent. It is a member of pyridines and an organobromine compound.
