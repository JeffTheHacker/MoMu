3-(4-Methoxyphenyl)-3, 4-dihydro-2H-1-benzopyran-7, 8-diol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 4'-o-methylated isoflavonoids. These are isoflavonoids with methoxy groups attached to the C4' atom of the isoflavonoid backbone. Isoflavonoids are natural products derived from 3-phenylchromen-4-one. 3-(4-Methoxyphenyl)-3, 4-dihydro-2H-1-benzopyran-7, 8-diol is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
