These are terpenes with three consecutive isoprene units. 3, 11, 12-Trihydroxy-1(10)-spirovetiven-2-one is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 3, 11, 12-trihydroxy-1(10)-spirovetiven-2-one is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Outside of the human body, 3, 11, 12-trihydroxy-1(10)-spirovetiven-2-one can be found in alcoholic beverages and potato. This makes 3, 11, 12-trihydroxy-1(10)-spirovetiven-2-one a potential biomarker for the consumption of these food products.
