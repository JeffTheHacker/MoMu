S-(2-Furanylmethyl) methanethioate, also known as it or fema 3158, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as heteroaromatic compounds. Heteroaromatic compounds are compounds containing an aromatic ring where a carbon atom is linked to an hetero atom. S-(2-Furanylmethyl) methanethioate is slightly soluble (in water) and an extremely weak basic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, S-(2-furanylmethyl) methanethioate is primarily located in the cytoplasm. S-(2-Furanylmethyl) methanethioate has a butterscotch, coffee, and horseradish taste.
