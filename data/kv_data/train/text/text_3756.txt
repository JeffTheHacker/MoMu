It is a benzo[a]quinolizidine alkaloid isolated from the Indian medicinal plant Alangium lamarckii. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of isoquinolines, a member of phenols, a tertiary amino compound, an aromatic ether, a primary alcohol, an isoquinoline alkaloid and a pyridoisoquinoline.
