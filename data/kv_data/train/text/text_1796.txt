cis-2, 3-Dimethylthiirane, also known as cis-2, 3-dimethylepisulfide or cis-2-butene episulfide, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as thiiranes. These are heterocyclic compounds containing a saturated three-member ring with two carbon atoms and one sulfur atom. Within the cell, cis-2, 3-dimethylthiirane is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
