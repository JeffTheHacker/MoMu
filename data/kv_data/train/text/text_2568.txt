2-(2-amino-3-methoxyphenyl)chromen-4-one is a member of the class of monomethoxyflavones that is 3'-methoxyflavone bearing an additional amino substituent at position 2' It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.24 (mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a monomethoxyflavone and an aromatic amine.
