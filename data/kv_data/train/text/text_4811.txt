It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.1 (non-specific serine/threonine protein kinase) inhibitor and an antineoplastic agent. It is an imidazopyridine, a member of piperidines, a 1,2,5-oxadiazole, an aromatic ether, a tertiary alcohol, an acetylenic compound, an aromatic amine and a primary amino compound.
