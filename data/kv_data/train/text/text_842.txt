Anthra[1,9-cd]pyrazol-6(2H)-one is a member of the class of anthrapyrazoles that is anthra[1,9-cd]pyrazole substituted at position 6 by an oxo group. An inhibitor of c-Jun N-terminal kinase. It has a role as a c-Jun N-terminal kinase inhibitor. It is an anthrapyrazole, a cyclic ketone and an aromatic ketone.
