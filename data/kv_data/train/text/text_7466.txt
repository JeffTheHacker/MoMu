Dethiobiotin is a hexanoic acid having a 5-methyl-2-oxoimidazolidin-4-yl group at the 6-position. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a member of ureas and an imidazolidinone. It derives from a hexanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a dethiobiotin(1-) and a (4R,5S)-dethiobiotin(1-).
