Ceftaroline is a cephalosporin that is the active metabolite of the prodrug ceftaroline fosamil. Used for the treatment of adults with acute bacterial skin and skin structure infections. It has a role as an antibacterial drug, an antimicrobial agent and a drug metabolite. It is a member of 1,3-thiazoles, a cephalosporin, an iminium betaine, an oxime O-ether and a member of thiadiazoles.
