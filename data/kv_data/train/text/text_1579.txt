Cyanidin 3-O-beta-D-glucoside is an anthocyanin cation that is a cyanidin cation linked to a beta-D-glucosyl moiety at position 3. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an anthocyanin cation, a beta-D-glucoside and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from a cyanidin cation. It is a conjugate acid of a cyanidin 3-O-beta-D-glucoside betaine.
