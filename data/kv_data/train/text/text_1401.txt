6-O-phosphono-D-glucono-1,5-lactone is an aldonolactone phosphate comprising D-glucono-1,5-lactone having the phosphate group at the 6-position. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It derives from a D-glucono-1,5-lactone. It is a conjugate acid of a 6-O-phosphonato-D-glucono-1,5-lactone(2-).
