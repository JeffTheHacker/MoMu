19-hydroxy-5alpha-dihydrotestosterone is an androstanoid that is 5alpha-dihydrotestosterone carrying an additional hydroxy susbstituent at position 19. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite. It is a 19-hydroxy steroid, a 3-oxo-5alpha-steroid, an androstanoid and a 17beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a 17beta-hydroxy-5alpha-androstan-3-one. It derives from a hydride of a 5alpha-androstane.
