Used as a solvent, fumigant, in the manufacture of other chemicals, and for many other uses. it is a member of the class of chloroethenes that is ethene substituted by chloro groups at positions 1, 1 and 2. It has a role as an inhalation anaesthetic and a mouse metabolite. it is a synthetic, light sensitive, volatile, colorless, liquid that is miscible with many non-polar organic solvents. it is used mainly as a degreaser for metal parts. Upon combustion, it produces irritants and toxic gases. Occupational exposure to it is associated with excess incidences of liver cancer, kidney cancer and non-Hodgkin lymphoma. It is reasonably anticipated to be a human carcinogen. (NCI05)
