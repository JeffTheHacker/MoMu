2, 3-Diketogulonate, also known as 2, 3-dioxo-L-gulonic, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as sugar acids and derivatives. Sugar acids and derivatives are compounds containing a saccharide unit which bears a carboxylic acid group. 2, 3-Diketogulonate is soluble (in water) and a moderately acidic compound (based on its pKa).
