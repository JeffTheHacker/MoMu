5alpha-androstane-3beta,6alpha,17beta-triol is a 3beta sterol that is 5alpha-androstane which is substituted by beta-hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 17 and by an alpha-hydroxy group at position 6. It has a role as a mammalian metabolite. It is a triol, a 6alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 3beta-sterol and a 17beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a 5alpha-androstane.
