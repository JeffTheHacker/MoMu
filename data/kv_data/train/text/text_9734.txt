3-(3, 4-Dihydroxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxycinnamic acids. Hydroxycinnamic acids are compounds containing an cinnamic acid where the benzene ring is hydroxylated. 3-(3, 4-Dihydroxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid is slightly soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). 3-(3, 4-Dihydroxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid can be converted into caffeoyl-CoA and alkyl caffeate ester.
