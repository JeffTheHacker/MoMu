(-)-it is an Aspidosperma alkaloid that is (5alpha,12beta,19alpha)-2,3-didehydroaspidospermidine which is substituted by a methoxycarbonyl group at position 3 and by an acetoxy group at position 20. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an Aspidosperma alkaloid, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, an acetate ester, a methyl ester, a tertiary amino compound and an enamine. It is a conjugate base of a (-)-it(1+).
