2-amino-4-(3-cyclohexen-1-yl)-7,7-dimethyl-5-oxo-5,6,7,8-tetrahydrochromene-3-carbonitrile is a member of the class of chromenes that is 7,7-dimethyl-5-oxo-5,6,7,8-tetrahydrochromene carrying additional amino, cyano and 3-cyclohexen-1-yl substituents at positions 2, 3 and 4 respectively. It is an enone, an enamine, an aliphatic nitrile, a cyclic ketone and a member of chromenes.
