(2S)-1-oleoyl-2-methylglycero-3-phosphothionate is a glycerophospholipid obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of oleic acid with the primary hydroxy group of (2S)-2-methylglycero-3-phosphothionate. It has a role as a lysophosphatidic acid receptor 3 agonist. It is an organic thiophosphate, a glycerophospholipid and an ether lipid. It derives from an oleic acid.
