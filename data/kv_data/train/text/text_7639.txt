3-(3, 5-Dimethoxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as coumaric acids and derivatives. These are aromatic compounds containing Aromatic compounds containing a cinnamic acid moiety (or a derivative thereof) hydroxylated at the C2 (ortho-), C3 (meta-), or C4 (para-) carbon atom of the benzene ring. 3-(3, 5-Dimethoxyphenyl)prop-2-enoic acid is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
