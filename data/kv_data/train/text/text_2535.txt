2-Propenyl 2-furancarboxylate, also known as 2-furoic acid, allyl ester or 2-propen-1-yl 2-furoate, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as furoic acid esters. These are ester derivatives of furoic acid. 2-Propenyl 2-furancarboxylate is slightly soluble (in water) and an extremely weak basic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2-propenyl 2-furancarboxylate is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2-Propenyl 2-furancarboxylate has a caramel, fruity, and jam taste.
