Isoleucyl-valine, also known as it or ile-val, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as dipeptides. These are organic compounds containing a sequence of exactly two alpha-amino acids joined by a peptide bond. Isoleucyl-valine is slightly soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Isoleucyl-valine has been primarily detected in feces.
