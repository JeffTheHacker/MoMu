4-(Prop-2-en-1-yl)benzene-1, 2-diol, also known as it, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as catechols. Catechols are compounds containing a 1, 2-benzenediol moiety. 4-(Prop-2-en-1-yl)benzene-1, 2-diol is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
