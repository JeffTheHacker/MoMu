Propyl propane thiosulfonate belongs to the class of organic compounds known as dicarboxylic acids and derivatives. These are organic compounds containing exactly two carboxylic acid groups. Propyl propane thiosulfonate is slightly soluble (in water) and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, propyl propane thiosulfonate is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Propyl propane thiosulfonate has an alliaceous and roasted taste.
