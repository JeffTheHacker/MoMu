2, 3, 6-Trimethylphenol, also known as it or fema 3963, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as ortho cresols. These are organic compounds containing an ortho-cresol moiety, which consists of a benzene bearing one hydroxyl group at ring positions 1 and 2, respectively. 2, 3, 6-Trimethylphenol exists as a solid, slightly soluble (in water), and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
