2-Hexyl-1, 3-dioxan-5-ol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 1, 3-dioxanes. These are organic compounds containing 1, 3-dioxane, an aliphatic six-member ring with two oxygen atoms in ring positions 1 and 3. 2-Hexyl-1, 3-dioxan-5-ol is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2-hexyl-1, 3-dioxan-5-ol is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
