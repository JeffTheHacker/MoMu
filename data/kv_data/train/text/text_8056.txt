Diuron-desmethyl is a member of the class of phenylureas that is urea in which a hydrogens attached to one nitrogen is substituted by a methyl group, and one of the hydrogens attached to the other nitrogen is substituted by a 3,4-dichlorophenyl group. It is a metabolite of diuron. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a dichlorobenzene and a member of phenylureas.
