it is a benzamide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 4-aminobenzoic acid with the primary amino group of 1-(2-aminoethyl)-2-cyano-3-{3-[3-(piperidin-1-ylmethyl)phenoxy]propyl}guanidine. It has a role as a H2-receptor antagonist. It is a member of guanidines, a member of benzamides, a substituted aniline, a nitrile, an aromatic ether and a member of piperidines.
