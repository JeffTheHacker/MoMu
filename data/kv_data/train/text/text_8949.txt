2-{[hydroxy(3-methoxyphenyl)methylidene]amino}acetic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hippuric acids. Hippuric acids are compounds containing hippuric acid, which consists of a of a benzoyl group linked to the N-terminal of a glycine. 2-{[hydroxy(3-methoxyphenyl)methylidene]amino}acetic acid is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
