It has a role as a chelator, an antirheumatic drug and a drug allergen. It is a it and a non-proteinogenic alpha-amino acid. It is an enantiomer of a L-it. it is a beta dimethyl analog of the amino acid cysteine. As a degradation product of penicillin antibiotics, it chelates with heavy metals and increases their urinary excretion. Possessing antineoplastic properties, it induces apoptosis by a p53-mediated mechanism and inhibits angiogenesis by chelating with copper, a cofactor for angiogenesis. (NCI04)
