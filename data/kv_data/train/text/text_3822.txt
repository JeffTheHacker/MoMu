Not registered as a pesticide in the U.S. (EPA, 1998) it is an organothiophosphate insecticide and an organic thiophosphate. It has a role as an agrochemical and an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor. It derives from a 4-cyanophenol.
