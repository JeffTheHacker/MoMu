1-Phenyl-2, 4-pentadiyn-1-one, also known as demethylcapillone or it, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as benzoyl derivatives. These are organic compounds containing an acyl moiety of benzoic acid with the formula (C6H5CO-). 1-Phenyl-2, 4-pentadiyn-1-one exists as a solid and is considered to be practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral. Within the cell, 1-phenyl-2, 4-pentadiyn-1-one is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Outside of the human body, 1-phenyl-2, 4-pentadiyn-1-one can be found in herbs and spices.
