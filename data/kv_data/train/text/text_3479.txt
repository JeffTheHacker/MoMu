S-it belongs to the class of organic compounds known as phenylhydantoins. These are heterocyclic aromatic compounds containing an imiazolidinedione moiety substituted by a phenyl group. S-it is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). S-it has been found in human liver and kidney tissues, and has also been detected in multiple biofluids, such as urine and blood.
