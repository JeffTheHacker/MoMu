it is an organic thiophosphate that is the O-ethyl-S,S-diphenyl ester of phosphorodithioic acid. Used to control a variety of fungal diseases on rice including blast, ear blight and stem rot. it is moderately toxic to mammals and fish but poses more of a risk to aquatic invertebrates. It has a role as a neurotoxin, an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor, a phospholipid biosynthesis inhibitor and an antifungal agrochemical.
