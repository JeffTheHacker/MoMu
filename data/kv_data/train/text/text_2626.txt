Slightly viscous, colorless and odorless liquid cyclic ether. it is used as a stabilizer for natural oils and vinyl polymers, as an alkylating agent, a sterilant, a demulsifier, a dye-leveling agent and it is also used as an intermediate in the synthesis of glycerol, glycidyl ethers and amines. Exposure to it irritates the skin, eyes, and mucous membranes and depresses the central nervous system in humans. it induces mutations in human lymphocytes and is reasonably anticipated to be a human carcinogen.
