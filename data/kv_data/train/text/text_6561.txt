13-hydroperoxyoctadeca-9, 11-dienoic acid is classified as a lineolic acids or a derivative. A lineolic acids or a derivative are derivatives of lineolic acid. Lineolic acid is a polyunsaturated omega-6 18 carbon long fatty acid, with two CC double bonds at the 9- and 12-positions. 13-hydroperoxyoctadeca-9, 11-dienoic acid is considered a practically insoluble (in water), acidic compound.
