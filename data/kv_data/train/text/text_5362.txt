4-hydroxy-3-methoxybenzene-1-sulfonic acid is an arenesulfonic acid that is guaiacol sulfated at position 4. Commonly used (in the form of its potassium salt) as an expectorant. It has a role as an expectorant. It is an arenesulfonic acid and a member of guaiacols. It derives from a guaiacol. It is a conjugate acid of a 4-hydroxy-3-methoxybenzene-1-sulfonate.
