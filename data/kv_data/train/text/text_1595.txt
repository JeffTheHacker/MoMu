It acts as a proteasome inhibitor and is isolated from Apiospora montagnei Sacc. TC 1093, isolated from a soil sample. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent, an antineoplastic agent, a proteasome inhibitor and a fungal metabolite. It is a member of indoles, a lactam, a macrocycle, a member of phenols, a secondary alcohol and a tertiary alcohol.
