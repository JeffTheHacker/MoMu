it is an N-acylpiperidine that is N-acetyl 4-(1,3-thiazol-2-yl)piperidine in which the thiazole ring is substituted at position 4 by a 5-(2,6-difluorophenyl)-4,5-dihydro-1,2-oxazol-3-yl group and in which one of the hydrogens of the acetyl group is replaced by a 5-methyl-3-(trifluoromethyl)-1H-pyrazol-1-yl group. It is a N-acylpiperidine, a member of 1,3-thiazoles, a member of pyrazoles, an organofluorine compound, an isoxazoline and a tertiary carboxamide.
