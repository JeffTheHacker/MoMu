A phosphodiesterase 4 inhibitor that is used for treatment of mild to moderate atopic dermatitis in children and adults. It has a role as a phosphodiesterase IV inhibitor, an antipsoriatic and a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug. It is a benzoxaborole, an aromatic ether and a nitrile.
