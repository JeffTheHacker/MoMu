2, 4-Dimethyl-1, 3-dioxolane belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 1, 3-dioxolanes. These are organic compounds containing 1, 3-dioxolane, an aliphatic five-member ring with two oxygen atoms in ring positions 1 and 3. 2, 4-Dimethyl-1, 3-dioxolane is soluble (in water) and an extremely weak basic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2, 4-dimethyl-1, 3-dioxolane is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2, 4-Dimethyl-1, 3-dioxolane has a fruity, grassy, and green taste.
