Methoxyphenols are compounds containing a methoxy group attached to the benzene ring of a phenol moiety. 2-Methoxy-4-(4-methyl-1, 3-dioxolan-2-yl)phenol is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2-methoxy-4-(4-methyl-1, 3-dioxolan-2-yl)phenol is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2-Methoxy-4-(4-methyl-1, 3-dioxolan-2-yl)phenol has a sweet, chocolate, and creamy taste.
