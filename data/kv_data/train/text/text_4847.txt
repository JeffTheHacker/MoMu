4-thiapentanal appears as a colorless to amber liquid with an extremely foul and persistent odor. Slightly soluble in water and denser than water. Contact may slightly irritate skin, eyes and mucous membranes. Moderately toxic. Used as a food additive. 3-methylthiopropanal is an aliphatic sulfide. It has a role as a prostaglandin antagonist. It derives from a propanal.
