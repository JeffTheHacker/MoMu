2, 4-Dimethylthiazole belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 2, 4-disubstituted thiazoles. 2, 4-disubstituted thiazoles are compounds containing a thiazole ring substituted at the positions 2 and 3. 2, 4-Dimethylthiazole is slightly soluble (in water) and a strong basic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2, 4-dimethylthiazole is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2, 4-Dimethylthiazole is a barley, beefy, and coffee tasting compound that can be found in tea. This makes 2, 4-dimethylthiazole a potential biomarker for the consumption of this food product.
