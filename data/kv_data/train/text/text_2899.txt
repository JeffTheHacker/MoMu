1, 2-Benzenediol bis(trimethylsilyl) ether belongs to the class of organic compounds known as phenoxy compounds. These are aromatic compounds contaning a phenoxy group. 1, 2-Benzenediol bis(trimethylsilyl) ether is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. 1, 2-Benzenediol bis(trimethylsilyl) ether has been primarily detected in feces.
