P,p&#39;-dde appears as white crystalline solid or white powder. (NTP, 1992) DDE is a chlorophenylethylene that is ethylene substituted by two 4-chlorophenyl groups at position 1 and two chlorine atoms at position 2. It has a role as a persistent organic pollutant and a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a member of monochlorobenzenes and a chlorophenylethylene.
