11,14,15-trihydroxy-(5Z,8Z,12E)-icosatrienoic acid is a trihydroxyicosatrienoic acid that is (5Z,8Z,12E)-icosatrienoic acid in which the three hydroxy groups are located at positions 11, 14 and 15. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is a trihydroxyicosatrienoic acid and a secondary allylic alcohol. It is a conjugate acid of an 11,14,15-trihydroxy-(5Z,8Z,12E)-icosatrienoate(1-).
