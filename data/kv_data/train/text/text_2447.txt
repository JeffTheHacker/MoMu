Hamacanthin A is a member of the class of pyrazines that is 5,6-dihydropyrazin-2(1H)-one substituted at positions 3 and 6 by 6-bromo-1H-indol-3-yl groups. It is an antifungal drug isolated from deep water marine sponge Hamacantha sp. It has a role as a metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is an indole alkaloid, an organobromine compound, a member of pyrazines and a lactam.
