N-it is a colorless liquid. Used in organic chemical synthesis, plasticizers, detergents, etc. it is a primary alcohol that is butane in which a hydrogen of one of the methyl groups is substituted by a hydroxy group. It it produced in small amounts in humans by the gut microbes. It has a role as a protic solvent, a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a primary alcohol and an alkyl alcohol.
