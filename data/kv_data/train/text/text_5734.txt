1,2-di-[(9Z)-hexadecenoyl]-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which both acyl substituents are specified as (9Z)-hexadecenoyl (palmitoleoyl). It derives from a palmitoleic acid. It is a tautomer of a 1,2-di-[(9Z)-hexadecenoyl]-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
