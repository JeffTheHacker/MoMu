2-O-Methyl-D-xylose belongs to the class of organic compounds known as pentoses. These are monosaccharides in which the carbohydrate moiety contains five carbon atoms. 2-O-Methyl-D-xylose exists as a solid, soluble (in water), and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Outside of the human body, 2-O-methyl-D-xylose can be found in fruits. This makes 2-O-methyl-D-xylose a potential biomarker for the consumption of this food product.
