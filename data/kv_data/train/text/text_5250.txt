3, 5-Dimethyl-2-(2-propenyl)pyrazine, also known as 2-allyl-3, 5-dimethylpyrazine, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as pyrazines. Pyrazines are compounds containing a pyrazine ring, which is a six-member aromatic heterocycle, that consists of two nitrogen atoms (at positions 1 and 4) and four carbon atoms. 3, 5-Dimethyl-2-(2-propenyl)pyrazine is slightly soluble (in water) and a moderately basic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 3, 5-dimethyl-2-(2-propenyl)pyrazine is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
