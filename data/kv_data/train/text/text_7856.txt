2-Isopropyl-1, 4-benzenediol, also known as 1, 4-dihydroxy-2-isopropylbenzene or 2, 5-dihydroxycumene, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as cumenes. These are aromatic compounds containing a prop-2-ylbenzene moiety. 2-Isopropyl-1, 4-benzenediol exists as a solid, slightly soluble (in water), and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2-isopropyl-1, 4-benzenediol is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
