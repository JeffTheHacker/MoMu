3-Ethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione, also known as fema 3152, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as cyclic ketones. These are organic compounds containing a ketone that is conjugated to a cyclic moiety. 3-Ethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione is soluble (in water) and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Outside of the human body, 3-ethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione can be found in coffee and coffee products. This makes 3-ethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione a potential biomarker for the consumption of this food product.
