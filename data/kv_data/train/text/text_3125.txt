Iodine atom is chemical element with atomic number 53. It has a role as a micronutrient and a human metabolite. Iodine I-125 is a radioactive isotope of iodine, a nonmetallic element of the halogen group. With a half-life of 60 days, iodine 125 occurs naturally and can be produced artificially. This agent has both therapeutic and diagnostic uses, particularly in thyroid disease. (NCI04)
