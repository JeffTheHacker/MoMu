5-Hydroxy-2-benzyl-1, 3-dioxane, also known as 2-benzyl-1, 3-dioxan-5-ol, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 1, 3-dioxanes. These are organic compounds containing 1, 3-dioxane, an aliphatic six-member ring with two oxygen atoms in ring positions 1 and 3. 5-Hydroxy-2-benzyl-1, 3-dioxane is soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 5-hydroxy-2-benzyl-1, 3-dioxane is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
