1-(4-chlorophenoxy)-3-{[2-(4-ethoxyphenyl)-3,3,3-trifluoropropoxy]methyl}benzene is an aromatic ether that is [3-(4-chlorophenoxy)phenyl]methanol in which the hydroxy group is replaced by a 2-(4-ethoxyphenyl)-3,3,3-trifluoropropoxy group. It is an aromatic ether, an organofluorine compound and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
