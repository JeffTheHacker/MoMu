Rhamnacene is a dimethoxyflavone that is quercetin in which the hydroxy groups at the 3' and 7 positions have been replaced by methoxy groups. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a dimethoxyflavone, a trihydroxyflavone, an aromatic ether and a member of phenols. It derives from a quercetin.
