Ethorop is one of a family of organophosphorus pesticides. It is combustible though it may require some effort to ignite. It is very toxic by skin absorption and inhalation. It may or may not be water soluble. it is an organic thiophosphate and an organothiophosphate insecticide. It has a role as an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor, an agrochemical and an antinematodal drug.
