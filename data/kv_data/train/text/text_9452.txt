Cholesta-5,7-dien-3beta,25-diol is a cholestanoid that is 7-dehydrocholesterol carrying an additional hydroxy substituent at position 25. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a 25-hydroxy steroid, a cholestanoid, a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid and a C27-steroid. It derives from a cholesta-5,7-dien-3beta-ol.
