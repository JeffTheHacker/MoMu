2-methoxy-17beta-estradiol 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide) is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is 2-methoxy-17beta-estradiol having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 3. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, an aromatic ether, a 17beta-hydroxy steroid and a steroid glucosiduronic acid. It derives from a 2-methoxy-17beta-estradiol. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-methoxy-17beta-estradiol 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
