Heptan-2-yl [(5-chloroquinolin-8-yl)oxy]acetate is a member of the class of quinolines that is quinoline which is substituted by a chloro group at position 5 and by a 2-[(heptan-2-yl)oxy]-2-oxoethoxy group at position 8. It is an aromatic ether, an organochlorine compound, a member of quinolines and a carboxylic ester.
