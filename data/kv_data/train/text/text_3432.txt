It can be found in red wine, in barley, in beer, in peach or in Jatropha macrantha, the Huanarpo Macho. It has a role as a metabolite, an antioxidant, an anti-inflammatory agent and an EC 2.3.1.48 (histone acetyltransferase) inhibitor. It is a hydroxyflavan, a proanthocyanidin, a biflavonoid and a polyphenol. It derives from a (+)-catechin.
