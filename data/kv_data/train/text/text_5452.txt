Vinblastine sulfate appears as an anticancer drug. White to slightly yellow crystalline powder. (NTP, 1992) Vinblastine Sulfate is the sulfate salt of vinblastine, a natural alkaloid isolated from the plant Catharanthus roseus (Madagascar periwinkle) with antineoplastic properties. Vinblastine disrupts microtubule formation and function during mitosis and interferes with glutamic acid metabolism. (NCI04)
