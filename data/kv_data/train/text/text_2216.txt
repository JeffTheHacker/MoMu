N(2)-(3,4-dichlorobenzoyl)-N,N-dipentyl-alpha-glutamine is a dicarboxylic acid monoamide obtained by formal condensation of the alpha-carboxy group of N-(3,4-dichlorobenzoyl)glutamic acid with the amino group of dipentylamine. It is a glutamic acid derivative, a dicarboxylic acid monoamide, a member of benzamides and a dichlorobenzene.
