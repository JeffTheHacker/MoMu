8-(1, 2-Dihydroxy-3-methylbut-3-en-1-yl)-7-methoxy-2H-chromen-2-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as coumarins and derivatives. These are polycyclic aromatic compounds containing a 1-benzopyran moiety with a ketone group at the C2 carbon atom (1-benzopyran-2-one). 8-(1, 2-Dihydroxy-3-methylbut-3-en-1-yl)-7-methoxy-2H-chromen-2-one is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
