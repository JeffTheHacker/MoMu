3-Methylbutyl 3-oxobutanoate, also known as 3-methylbutyl acetoacetate or it, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as beta-keto acids and derivatives. These are organic compounds containing an aldehyde substituted with a keto group on the C3 carbon atom. 3-Methylbutyl 3-oxobutanoate is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). 3-Methylbutyl 3-oxobutanoate has a sweet, ethereal, and fermented taste.
