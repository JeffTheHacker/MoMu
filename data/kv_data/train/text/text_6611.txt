5-[(E)-caffeoyl]shikimate is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion that is the conjugate base of 5-[(E)-caffeoyl]shikimic acid. Major structure at pH 7.3 It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a cyclohexenecarboxylate and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 5-[(E)-caffeoyl]shikimic acid.
