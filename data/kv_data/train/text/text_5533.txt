Benzene-1, 2, 3, 4, 5-pentol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 5-unsubstituted pyrrogallols. These are pyrrogallols that are unsubstituted at th5-position of the benzene ring. Benzene-1, 2, 3, 4, 5-pentol is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
