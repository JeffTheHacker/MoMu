It has a role as a persistent organic pollutant. it is a white, stable, odorless, synthetic, crystalline solid chlorinated hydrocarbon. it was used as an insecticide against ants and as a fire-retardant for plastics, paint, rubber, paper and electrical appliances, but it is no longer produced or used in the US. Exposure to it irritates the skin and eyes and causes a headache as well as dizziness, nausea and vomiting and affects the central nervous system, lungs, liver and kidneys. it is reasonably anticipated to be a human carcinogen.
