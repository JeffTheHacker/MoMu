2-(4-iodo-2,5-dimethoxyphenyl)-1-methylethylamine is an organoiodine compound that is amphetamine bearing two methoxy substituents at positions 2 and 5 as well as an iodo substituent at position 4. It is an organoiodine compound, a dimethoxybenzene and a member of amphetamines. It derives from an amphetamine.
