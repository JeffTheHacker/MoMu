Nitroanisole appears as a light red or amber-colored liquid or crystals. Insoluble in water and denser than water. Contact may irritate skin, eyes and mucous membranes. May be toxic by ingestion, inhalation or skin absorption. Used to make other chemicals. it is a member of the class of its that is anisole in which one the hydrogen meta to the methoxy group is replaced by a nitro group.
