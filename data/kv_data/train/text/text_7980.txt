Creosote, beechwood is a colorless to yellowish oily liquid with a smoky odor and caustic burning taste. (NTP, 1992) it is a monomethoxybenzene that consists of phenol with a methoxy substituent at the ortho position. It has a role as an expectorant, a disinfectant, a plant metabolite and an EC 1.1.1.25 (shikimate dehydrogenase) inhibitor. It derives from a catechol.
