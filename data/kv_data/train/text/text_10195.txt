(4Z,8E,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-7-hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid is a hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid that consists of (4Z,8E,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid bearing an additional 7-hydroxy substituent. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,8E,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-7-hydroxydocosahexaenoate.
