5S, 6S-Epoxy-15R-hydroxy-ete belongs to the class of organic compounds known as polyethylene glycols. These are oligomers or polymers of ethylene oxide, with the general formula (C2H4O)n (with n>=3). 5S, 6S-Epoxy-15R-hydroxy-ete exists as a solid, soluble (in water), and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa).
