it is a sulfonamide resulting from the formal condensation of the sulfonic acid group of mesitylene-2-sulphonic acid with the amino group of 2-(m-bromophenyl)ethylamine in which the nitrogen is substituted by a 4-[m-(methylsulfonyl)phenyl]benzyl group. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an apoptosis inducer and a liver X receptor inverse agonist. It is a sulfonamide, a sulfone and a member of bromobenzenes.
