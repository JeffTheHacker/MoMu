it is any of a group of glycosaminoglycans with repeating units consisting of variously sulfated beta1->4-linked L-iduronyl-(alpha1->3)-N-acetyl-D-galactosamine units. It has a role as a hematologic agent and an anticoagulant. It is a sulfated glycosaminoglycan and a mucopolysaccharide. It derives from a dermatan. It is a conjugate acid of a it polyanion.
