2, 6-Dimethyl-7-octene-2, 6-diol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as tertiary alcohols. Tertiary alcohols are compounds in which a hydroxy group, -OH, is attached to a saturated carbon atom R3COH (R not H ). 2, 6-Dimethyl-7-octene-2, 6-diol exists as a solid, slightly soluble (in water), and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2, 6-dimethyl-7-octene-2, 6-diol is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Outside of the human body, 2, 6-dimethyl-7-octene-2, 6-diol can be found in alcoholic beverages and fruits. This makes 2, 6-dimethyl-7-octene-2, 6-diol a potential biomarker for the consumption of these food products.
