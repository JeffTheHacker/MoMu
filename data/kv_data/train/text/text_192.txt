Versiconol acetate is an acetate ester that is the O-acetyl derivative of versiconol. An intermediate in the biosynthesis of aflatoxin. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a polyphenol, an acetate ester and a tetrahydroxyanthraquinone. It is a conjugate acid of a versiconol acetate(1-).
