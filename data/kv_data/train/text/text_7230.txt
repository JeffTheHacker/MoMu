A fungicide used to control diseases caused by Basidomycetes including Rhizoctonia and Puccinia spp. It has a role as an EC 1.3.5.1 [succinate dehydrogenase (quinone)] inhibitor and an antifungal agrochemical. It is a member of benzamides, an aromatic ether and a benzanilide fungicide.
