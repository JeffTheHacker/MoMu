it8 is a heterodetic cyclic peptide consisting of somatostatin in which the amino terminus has been acylated by the fourteen membered peptide Ser-Ala-Asn-Ser-Asn-Pro-Ala-Met-Ala-Pro-Arg-Glu-Arg-Lys. It has a role as a fungal metabolite, a rat metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a heterodetic cyclic peptide and a peptide hormone. It derives from a somatostatin.
