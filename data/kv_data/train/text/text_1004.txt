5-chloro-1,3-dimethyl-N-(1,1,3-trimethyl-1,3-dihydro-2-benzofuran-4-yl)pyrazole-4-carboxamide is an aromatic amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 5-chloro-1,3-dimethylpyrazole-4-carboxylic acid with the amino group of 1,1,3-trimethyl-1,3-dihydro-2-benzofuran-4-amine. It is an aromatic amide, a member of pyrazoles, an organochlorine compound and a member of 1-benzofurans.
