Stigmasterol 3-O-acetate is a steroid ester obtained by the formal condensation of the hydroxy group of phytosterol with acetic acid. It has been obtained from the mycelia of Cordyceps sinensis. It has a role as a fungal metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a steroid ester and an acetate ester. It derives from a stigmasterol. It derives from a hydride of a stigmastane.
