Ethyl 2-{4-[(6-chloroquinoxalin-2-yl)oxy]phenoxy}propanoate is an ethyl ester resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of 2-{4-[(6-chloroquinoxalin-2-yl)oxy]phenoxy}propanoic acid with ethanol. It is an ethyl ester, an organochlorine compound, a quinoxaline derivative and an aromatic ether. It derives from a 2-{4-[(6-chloroquinoxalin-2-yl)oxy]phenoxy}propanoic acid.
