Pyridinium-3,5-bisthiocarboxylic acid mononucleotide is a pyridine nucleotide having pyridinium-3,5-bisthiocarboxylic acid as the nucleobase. It is a nucleoside 5'-monophosphate, a pyridine nucleotide and a monothiocarboxylic acid. It derives from a nicotinic acid D-ribonucleotide. It is a conjugate acid of a pyridinium-3,5-bisthiocarboxylate mononucleotide(3-).
