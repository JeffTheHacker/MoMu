it is an azamacrocyclic compound that is a hepatitis C protease inhibitor used in combination with elbasvir (under the brand name Zepatier) for treatment of chronic HCV genotypes 1 or 4 infection in adults. It has a role as an antiviral drug, a hepatoprotective agent and a hepatitis C protease inhibitor. It is an azamacrocycle, a carbamate ester, a lactam, an aromatic ether, a member of cyclopropanes, a N-sulfonylcarboxamide and a quinoxaline derivative.
