4-hydroxy-3-nitrophenyl arsonic acid appears as pale yellow crystals or light tan powder. (NTP, 1992) it is an organoarsonic acid where the organyl group is 4-hydroxy-3-nitrophenyl. It has a role as a coccidiostat, an antibacterial drug, an agrochemical and an animal growth promotant. It is an organoarsonic acid and a member of 2-nitrophenols. It derives from a phenylarsonic acid.
