(2,4-dichlorophenyl)(phenyl)pyrimidin-5-ylmethanol is a member of the class of pyrimidines that is methanol in which the hydrogens of the methyl group have been replaced by pyrimidin-5-yl, phenyl, and 2,4-dichlorophenyl groups, respectively. It is a member of pyrimidines, a tertiary alcohol, an aromatic alcohol and a dichlorobenzene.
