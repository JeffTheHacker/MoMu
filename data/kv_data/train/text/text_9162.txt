N-isopropyl-n&#39;-phenyl-p-phenylenediamine appears as dark gray to black flakes or brown-black small chip-like solid with an aromatic odor. (NTP, 1992) it is the N-substituted diamine that is 1,4-phenylenediamine substituted at one N with an isopropyl group and at the other with a phenyl group. It has a role as an antioxidant and an allergen.
