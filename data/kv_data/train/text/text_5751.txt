ITP is the inosine phosphate that has a triphosphate group at the 5'-position. It is an intermediate in the metabolism of purine. It has a role as a human metabolite, a mouse metabolite and an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a purine ribonucleoside 5'-triphosphate and an inosine phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of an ITP(3-) and an ITP(4-).
