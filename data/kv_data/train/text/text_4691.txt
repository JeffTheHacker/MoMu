Oleoyl ethanolamide is an N-(long-chain-acyl)ethanolamine that is the ethanolamide of oleic acid. The monounsaturated analogue of the endocannabinoid anandamide It has a role as a PPARalpha agonist and an EC 3.5.1.23 (ceramidase) inhibitor. It is a N-(long-chain-acyl)ethanolamine, an endocannabinoid and a N-acylethanolamine 18:1. It derives from an oleic acid.
