Kaempferol 7-O-beta-D-glucopyranoside is a kaempferol O-glucoside that is kaempferol attached to a beta-D-glucopyranosyl moiety at position 7 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite, a radical scavenger and a plant metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a kaempferol O-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a trihydroxyflavone and a member of flavonols. It derives from a beta-D-glucose.
