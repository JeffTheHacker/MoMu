1, 2, 5-Trimethyl-1H-pyrrole, also known as pyrrole, 1, 2, 5-trimethyl, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as n-methylpyrroles. These are organic heterocyclic compounds containing a N-methylated pyrrole. Within the cell, 1, 2, 5-trimethyl-1H-pyrrole is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
