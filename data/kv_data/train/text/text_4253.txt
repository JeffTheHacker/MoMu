Estrone 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide) is the 3-beta-D-glucuronide of estrone. It has a role as a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a 17-oxo steroid, a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid and a steroid glucosiduronic acid. It derives from an estrone. It is a conjugate acid of an estrone 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
