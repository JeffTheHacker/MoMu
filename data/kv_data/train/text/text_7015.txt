Meso-2,6-diaminopimelic acid dizwitterion is dizwitterionic form of meso-2,6-diaminopimelic acid arising from migration of protons from both carboxy groups to the amino groups; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite and an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a meso-2,6-diaminopimelate(2-). It is a tautomer of a meso-2,6-diaminopimelic acid.
