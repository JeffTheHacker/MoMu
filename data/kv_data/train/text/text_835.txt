2, 5-Dimethyl-3(2H)-furanone, also known as 2, 5-dimethylfuran-3(2H)-one or 2H-furan-3-one, 2, 5-dimethyl, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as furanones. Furanones are compounds containing a furan ring bearing a ketone group. 2, 5-Dimethyl-3(2H)-furanone is soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2, 5-dimethyl-3(2H)-furanone is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2, 5-Dimethyl-3(2H)-furanone has a caramel, ester, and fruity taste.
