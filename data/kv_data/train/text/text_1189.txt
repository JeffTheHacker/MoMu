(NIOSH, 2016) 5-bromo-3-(butan-2-yl)-6-methylpyrimidine-2,4(1H,3H)-dione is a pyrimidone that is pyrimidine-2,4(1H,3H)-dione substituted by a bromo group at position 5, a butan-2-yl group at position 3 and a methyl group at position 6. It is a pyrimidone and an organobromine compound.
