1H-Indole, 3-methyl-1- (trimethylsilyl)- belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 3-methylindoles. These are aromatic heterocyclic compounds that contain an indole moiety substituted at the 3-position with a methyl group. 1H-Indole, 3-methyl-1- (trimethylsilyl)- is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
