Pyridine, 4-amino- is a white crystalline material with no odor. Used as an avicide, an intermediate and as a fixer for some textile dyes. (EPA, 1998)  It has been approved by the FDA for use as a treatment for multiple sclerosis. it is an aromatic amine that is pyridine bearing a single amino substituent at position 4. An orphan drug in the US, it is used to improve walking in adults with multiple sclerosis. It has a role as an avicide, a potassium channel blocker and an orphan drug. It is an aromatic amine and an aminopyridine.
