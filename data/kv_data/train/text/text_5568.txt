The disodium salt is the histological dye 'Congo corinth'. It has a role as a fluorochrome and a histological dye. It is an aminonaphthalenesulfonic acid, a member of azobenzenes, a bis(azo) compound, a member of naphthols and a ring assembly. It is a conjugate acid of a Congo corinth(2-).
