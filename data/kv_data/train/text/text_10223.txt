it, also known as it or O-formylanisole, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as benzoyl derivatives. These are organic compounds containing an acyl moiety of benzoic acid with the formula (C6H5CO-). it exists as a solid, slightly soluble (in water), and an extremely weak basic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, it is primarily located in the cytoplasm. it is a sweet, acetophenone, and almond tasting compound that can be found in chinese cinnamon and herbs and spices. This makes it a potential biomarker for the consumption of these food products.
