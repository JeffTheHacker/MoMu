it is a member of the class of pyrazoles that is 1H-pyrazole substituted by a methyl group at position 4. It has a role as an antidote, a protective agent and an EC 1.1.1.1 (alcohol dehydrogenase) inhibitor. It derives from a hydride of a 1H-pyrazole. it is a pyrazole with competitive alcohol dehydrogenase inhibitor activity. it prevents the metabolism of ethylene glycol and methanol by alcohol dehydrogenase, thereby inhibiting the formation of their toxic metabolites, glycolate and oxalate (from ethylene glycol), and formic acid (from methanol). it is indicated for use as an antidote in ethylene glycol and methanol poisoning.
