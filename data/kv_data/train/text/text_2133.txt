Repeating unit of poly(it), a biopolymer used by numerous bacterial species as carbon and energy reserves. It is a 3-hydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a 3-hydroxy fatty acid and a medium-chain fatty acid. It derives from an octanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-hydroxyoctanoate.
