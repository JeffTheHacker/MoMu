Iodocyanopindolol is an indole that is 1H-indole substituted at positions 2, 3 and 7 by cyano, iodo and 3-(tert-butylamino)-2-hydroxypropoxy groups respectively. It has a role as a beta-adrenergic antagonist and a serotonergic antagonist. It is a member of indoles, a nitrile, an organoiodine compound, an aromatic ether, a secondary alcohol and a secondary amino compound.
