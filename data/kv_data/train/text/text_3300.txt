3-Hydroxy-isoheptanoic acid, also known as 3-hydroxy-5-methylhexanoate, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as medium-chain hydroxy acids and derivatives. These are hydroxy acids with a 6 to 12 carbon atoms long side chain. 3-Hydroxy-isoheptanoic acid is soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
