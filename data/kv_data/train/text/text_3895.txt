HC toxin is a homodetic cyclic tetrapeptide made up from L-alanyl, D-alanyl, L-prolyl and 2-amino-8-oxo-9,10-epoxydecanoyl residues. It has a role as an EC 3.5.1.98 (histone deacetylase) inhibitor, a metabolite, a phytotoxin and an antineoplastic agent. It is a homodetic cyclic peptide and a tetrapeptide.
