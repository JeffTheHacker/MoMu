it is a member of the family of mitomycins that exhibits antibiotic and antitumour properties. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent and an antineoplastic agent. It is a mitomycin and an ether. It is a conjugate acid of a it(1-). it is a methylazirinopyrroloindoledione antineoplastic antibiotic isolated from the bacterium Streptomyces caespitosus. Bioreduced it generates oxygen radicals, alkylates DNA, and produces interstrand DNA cross-links, thereby inhibiting DNA synthesis. (NCI04)
