2-methoxyestrone 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide) is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is 2-methoxyestrone having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 3. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is a 17-oxo steroid, a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, an aromatic ether and a steroid glucosiduronic acid. It derives from a 2-methoxyestrone. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-methoxyestrone 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
