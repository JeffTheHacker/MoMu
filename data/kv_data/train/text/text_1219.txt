2, 4, 5-Trihydroxybenzoic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxybenzoic acid derivatives. Hydroxybenzoic acid derivatives are compounds containing a hydroxybenzoic acid (or a derivative), which is a benzene ring bearing a carboxyl and a hydroxyl groups. 2, 4, 5-Trihydroxybenzoic acid is slightly soluble (in water) and a moderately acidic compound (based on its pKa).
