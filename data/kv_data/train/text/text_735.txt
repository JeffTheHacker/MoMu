3-phosphoglyceric acid is a monophosphoglyceric acid having the phospho group at the 3-position. It is an intermediate in metabolic pathways like glycolysis and calvin cycle. It has a role as a fundamental metabolite and an algal metabolite. It is a tetronic acid derivative and a monophosphoglyceric acid. It derives from a glyceric acid. It is a conjugate acid of a it(3-), a it(2-) and a it.
