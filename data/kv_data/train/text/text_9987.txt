6,12-dimethyldibenzo[d,d']benzo[1,2-b:4,5-b']bisthiophene is an organic heteropentacyclic compound that is p-xylene in which the 2-3 and 5-6 bonds of the benzene ring have each been fused with the b edge of a benzothiophene moiety. It has a role as a mutagen. It is an organic heteropentacyclic compound and an organosulfur heterocyclic compound.
