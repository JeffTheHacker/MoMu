it is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is the 3-beta-D-glucuronide of estriol; a steroid hormone ligand recognised by the monoclonal antibody 4155. It has a role as an epitope and an estrogen. It is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, a 16alpha-hydroxy steroid and a steroid glucosiduronic acid. It derives from an estriol. It is a conjugate acid of an it(1-). It derives from a hydride of an estrane.
