(S)-(1-aminoethyl)phosphonic acid is a primary amino compound that is phosphonic acid having a 1-aminoethyl group attached to the phosphorus. It is a member of phosphonic acids and a primary amino compound. It derives from a phosphonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (S)-(1-aminoethyl)phosphonate. It is an enantiomer of a (R)-(1-aminoethyl)phosphonic acid.
