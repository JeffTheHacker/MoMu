4-(4-Hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl)but-3-en-2-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxycinnamic acids and derivatives. Hydroxycinnamic acids and derivatives are compounds containing an cinnamic acid (or a derivative thereof) where the benzene ring is hydroxylated. 4-(4-Hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl)but-3-en-2-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
