It is a selective inhibitor of the epsilon isoform of casein kinase 1 (CK1epsilon). It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.1 (non-specific serine/threonine protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a pyrazolopyrimidine, a member of monochlorobenzenes, an aromatic ether and a member of oxanes.
