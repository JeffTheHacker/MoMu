Cuelure, also known as fema 3652 or HDSF, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as phenol esters. These are aromatic compounds containing a benzene ring substituted by a hydroxyl group and an ester group. Cuelure is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, cuelure is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Cuelure has a berry, blueberry, and jam taste.
