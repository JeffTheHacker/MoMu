3-(3, 4-Dihydroxyphenyl)-1-phenylprop-2-en-1-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as retrochalcones. These are a form of normal chalcones that are structurally distinguished by the lack of oxygen functionalities at the C2'- and C6'-positions. 3-(3, 4-Dihydroxyphenyl)-1-phenylprop-2-en-1-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
