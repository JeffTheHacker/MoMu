16beta-hydroxyestradiol is a 3-hydroxy steroid that is 17beta-estradiol substituted by a beta-hydroxy group at position 16. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and an anti-inflammatory drug. It is a 16beta-hydroxy steroid, a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, a 3-hydroxy steroid and a member of phenols. It derives from a 17beta-estradiol. It derives from a hydride of an estrane.
