(NTP, 1992) Pontamine sky blue 5B is an organic sodium salt resulting from the formal condensation of Pontamine sky blue 5B (acid form) with four equivalents of sodium hydroxide. It has a role as a histological dye, a fluorochrome and a carcinogenic agent. It contains a Pontamine sky blue 5B(4-).
