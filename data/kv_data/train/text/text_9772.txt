Not registered as a pesticide in the U.S. (EPA, 1998) it is a carbamate ester. It has a role as an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor, a carbamate insecticide, an acaricide and an agrochemical. It derives from a methylcarbamic acid and a m-cresol.
