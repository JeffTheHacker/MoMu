5-Amino-1-(5-phosphonato-D-ribosyl)imidazol-3-ium belongs to the class of organic compounds known as pentose phosphates. These are carbohydrate derivatives containing a pentose substituted by one or more phosphate groups. 5-Amino-1-(5-phosphonato-D-ribosyl)imidazol-3-ium is slightly soluble (in water) and a moderately acidic compound (based on its pKa).
