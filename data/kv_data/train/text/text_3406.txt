2-{4-[(6-chloroquinoxalin-2-yl)oxy]phenoxy}propanoic acid is a monocarboxylic acid that is 2-phenoxypropanoic acid in which the phenyl group is substituted at the para position by a (6-chloroquinoxalin-2-yl)oxy group. It is a quinoxaline derivative, an aromatic ether, a monocarboxylic acid and an organochlorine compound.
