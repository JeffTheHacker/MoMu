It has been isolated from Glycyrrhiza uralensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an EC 1.14.18.1 (tyrosinase) inhibitor. It is a member of hydroxyisoflavans and a methoxyisoflavan. It derives from a hydride of a (R)-isoflavan.
