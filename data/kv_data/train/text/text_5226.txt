Ethyl 2-methyl-3, 4-pentadienoate, also known as fema 3678, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as fatty acid esters. These are carboxylic ester derivatives of a fatty acid. Ethyl 2-methyl-3, 4-pentadienoate is slightly soluble (in water) and an extremely weak basic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Ethyl 2-methyl-3, 4-pentadienoate has been primarily detected in urine. Within the cell, ethyl 2-methyl-3, 4-pentadienoate is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Ethyl 2-methyl-3, 4-pentadienoate has a sweet, fruity, and green taste.
