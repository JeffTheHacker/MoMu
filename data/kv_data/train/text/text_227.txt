Leucyl-glycine, also known as L-g dipeptide or leu-gly, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as dipeptides. These are organic compounds containing a sequence of exactly two alpha-amino acids joined by a peptide bond. Leucyl-glycine is soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Leucyl-glycine has been primarily detected in feces.
