2, 5-Bis(hydroxymethyl)oxolane-2, 3, 4-triol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as c-glycosyl compounds. These are glycoside in which a sugar group is bonded through one carbon to another group via a C-glycosidic bond. 2, 5-Bis(hydroxymethyl)oxolane-2, 3, 4-triol is very soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
