1-(4-chlorophenyl)-4,4-dimethyl-2-(1H-1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)pentan-3-ol is a member of the class of triazoles that is 2,2-dimethylpentane that is substituted at positions 2, 3, and 5 by hydroxy, 1,2,4-triazol-1-yl, and p-chlorophenyl groups, respectively. It is a member of triazoles, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a secondary alcohol.
