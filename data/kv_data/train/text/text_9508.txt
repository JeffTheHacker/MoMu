(Z)-3-(4-iodophenyl)-2-mercaptoacrylic acid is an organoiodine compound that is acrylic acid in which the vinylic hydrogens at positions 2 and 3 are replaced by mercapto and 4-iodophenyl groups respectively (the Z geoisomer). It has a role as a calpain inhibitor and an apoptosis inhibitor. It is an organoiodine compound, a member of cinnamic acids and a thioenol. It derives from a trans-cinnamic acid.
