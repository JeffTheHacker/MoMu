Crystals. Formerly used as a sedative and an anti-emetic. (EPA, 1998) it is a member of the class of isoxazoles that is 1,2-oxazol-3(2H)-one substituted by an aminomethyl group at position 5. It has been isolated from mushrooms of the genus Amanita. It has a role as a fungal metabolite, a GABA agonist, a psychotropic drug and a oneirogen. It is a member of isoxazoles, a primary amino compound and an alkaloid.
