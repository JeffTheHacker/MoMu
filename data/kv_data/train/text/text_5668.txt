Used for the control of Pyricularia oryzae on rice crops. It has a role as a mitochondrial cytochrome-bc1 complex inhibitor and an antifungal agrochemical. It is an oxime O-ether, a monocarboxylic acid amide, an aromatic ether, an amide fungicide and a methoxyiminoacetamide strobilurin antifungal agent.
