It has a role as an acaricide, an insecticide and an antifouling biocide. It is an organochlorine acaricide, an organochlorine insecticide, an organofluorine acaricide, an organofluorine insecticide, a member of pyrroles, a nitrile, a member of monochlorobenzenes and an organobromine compound.
