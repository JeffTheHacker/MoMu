it is a sulfonamide resulting from the formal condensation of the sulfonic acid group of 5-ethoxy-7-fluoro[1,2,4]triazolo[1,5-c]pyrimidine-2-sulfonic acid with the amino group of 2-amino-3-chlorobenzoic acid. The methyl ester of it, generally known as it-methyl, is used as a herbicide for the control of post-emergence control of broad-leaved weeds in soybeans. It has a role as a herbicide. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a sulfonamide, an aromatic ether, an organofluorine compound, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a member of triazolopyrimidines.
