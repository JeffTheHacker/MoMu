it is an anthracycline that is aklavinone having a 3-amino-2,3,6-trideoxy-alpha-L-lyxo-hexopyranosyl residue attached at position 4 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an aminoglycoside, a deoxy hexoside, a monosaccharide derivative, an anthracycline antibiotic, a member of tetracenequinones, a methyl ester and a member of p-quinones. It derives from an aklavinone. It is a conjugate base of a it(1+).
