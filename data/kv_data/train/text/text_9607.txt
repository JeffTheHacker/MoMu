3'-Carboxy-alpha-chromanol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 1-benzopyrans. These are organic aromatic compounds that 1-benzopyran, a bicyclic compound made up of a benzene ring fused to a pyran, so that the oxygen atom is at the 1-position. 3'-Carboxy-alpha-chromanol is soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
