It is isolated from the whole plant of Ballota limbata (Syn.Otostegia limbata) and acts as a lipoxygenase inhibitor. It has a role as a metabolite and a lipoxygenase inhibitor. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a diterpenoid, a diol, a carbobicyclic compound and a member of octahydronaphthalenes.
