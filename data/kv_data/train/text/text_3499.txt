1, 7-Diphenylheptan-3-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as linear diarylheptanoids. These are diarylheptanoids with an open heptane chain. The two aromatic rings are linked only by the heptane chain. 1, 7-Diphenylheptan-3-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
