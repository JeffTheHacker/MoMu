Naringenin 7-O-beta-D-glucuronide is a dihydroxyflavanone that is naringenin in which the phenolic hydrogen at position 7 has been replaced by a beta-D-glucuronosyl residue. It is a dihydroxyflavanone, a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, a polyphenol and a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones. It derives from a naringenin.
