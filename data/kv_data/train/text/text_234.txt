It is a pyridochromene and a monocarboxylic acid. it is an anti-aphthous ulcer drug. it inhibits the synthesis and release of inflammatory mediators, including leukotrienes and histamine, from mast cells, neutrophils, and mononuclear cells. it also acts as a leukotriene D4 antagonist and a phosphodiesterase inhibitor. it decreases the time ulcers take to heal as well as the pain associated with the ulcers.
