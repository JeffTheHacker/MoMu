It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an interferon inducer. it is a synthetic agent with immune response modifying activity. As an immune response modifier (IRM), it stimulates cytokine production, especially interferon production, and exhibits antitumor activity, particularly against cutaneous cancers. it's proapoptotic activity appears to be related to Bcl-2 overexpression in susceptible tumor cells.
