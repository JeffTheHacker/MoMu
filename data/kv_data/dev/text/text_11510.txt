A potent antagonist at adenosine A1 and adenosine A2A receptors. It has a role as an adenosine A1 receptor antagonist, an adenosine A2A receptor antagonist, a central nervous system stimulant and an antineoplastic agent. It is a triazoloquinazoline, an organochlorine compound, a member of furans, a biaryl, an aromatic amine and a primary amino compound.
