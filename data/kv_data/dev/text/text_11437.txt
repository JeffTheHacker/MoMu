2, 4-Dimethylpyridine, also known as 2, 4-lutidene, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as methylpyridines. These are organic compounds containing a pyridine ring substituted at one or more positions by a methyl group. 2, 4-Dimethylpyridine exists as a solid, soluble (in water), and a very strong basic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 2, 4-dimethylpyridine is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
