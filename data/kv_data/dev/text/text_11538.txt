P(1),P(4)-bis(5'-guanosyl) tetraphosphate is a purine ribonucleoside 5'-tetraphosphate compound having 5'-guanosyl residues at the P(1)- and P(4)-positions. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a guanosine 5'-phosphate and a purine ribonucleoside 5'-tetraphosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a P(1),P(4)-bis(5'-guanosyl) tetraphosphate(4-).
