Lissamine flavine FF is an organic sodium salt having 6-amino-2-(4-methylphenyl)-1,3-dioxo-2,3-dihydro-1H-benzo[de]isoquinoline-5-sulfonate as the counterion. It is used as the displacement dye in the yellowsolve I method for fibrin. It has a role as a histological dye. It contains a lissamine flavine FF(1-).
