Emits toxic fumes of nitrogen oxides and sulfur oxides when heated to decomposition. it is a synthetic, off-white to violet solid that is soluble in water and ethanol. It is used primarily as a component of oxidizing 'permanent' hair- and fur-dye formulations. it is also used as an intermediate in the production of C.I. Basic Brown 2, a dye commonly used in numerous consumer products. The primary routes of potential human exposure to it are dermal contact and inhalation.
