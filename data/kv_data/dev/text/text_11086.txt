it is a tetracycline analogue having a dimethylamino group at position 7 and lacking the methyl and hydroxy groups at position 5. It has a role as an antibacterial drug and an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a member of tetracyclines, a tetracenomycin and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It is a conjugate acid of a it(1-). It is a tautomer of a it zwitterion.
