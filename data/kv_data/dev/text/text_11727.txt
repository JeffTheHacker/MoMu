3-methyl-4'-dimethylaminoazobenzene is a member of the class of azobenzenes that is azobenzene in which one of the phenyl groups is substituted at position 3 by a methyl group, while the other is substituted at position 4 by a dimethylamino group. It is a potent liver carcinogen. It has a role as a carcinogenic agent. It is a member of azobenzenes and a tertiary amino compound.
