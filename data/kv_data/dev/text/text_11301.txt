5-oxo-L-proline is an optically active form of 5-oxoproline having L-configuration. It has a role as an algal metabolite. It is a L-proline derivative, a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid and a 5-oxoproline. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-oxo-L-prolinate. It is an enantiomer of a 5-oxo-D-proline. it is a keto derivative of the amino acid proline.
