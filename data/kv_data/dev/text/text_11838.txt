1, 2-Ethanedithiol, also known as ethylene dimercaptan or 1, 2-dimercaptoethane, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as alkylthiols. These are organic compounds containing the thiol functional group linked to an alkyl chain. 1, 2-Ethanedithiol exists as a solid, slightly soluble (in water), and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 1, 2-ethanedithiol is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 1, 2-Ethanedithiol has a sulfury taste.
