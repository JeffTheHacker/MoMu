1-O-all-trans-retinoyl-beta-glucuronic acid is a retinoid that is retinoic acid in which the carboxy proton has been replaced by a beta-D-glucuronyl residue. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a member of glucuronic acids and a retinoid. It derives from an all-trans-retinoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-O-(all-trans-retinoyl)-beta-D-glucuronate.
