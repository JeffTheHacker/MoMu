Produced by the gram-negative insect pathogens, Xenorhabdus nematophila and Photorhabdus luminescens, it is a potent nanomolar-level inhibitor of phenoloxidase, a key component of the insect's innate immune system. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite and an EC 1.14.18.1 (tyrosinase) inhibitor. It is an isocyanide, a member of acetamides and an olefinic compound.
