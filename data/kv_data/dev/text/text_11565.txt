(NTP, 1992) it is a sulfonamide consisting of pyridine with a 4-aminobenzenesulfonamido group at the 2-position. It has a role as an antiinfective agent, a dermatologic drug, a xenobiotic, an environmental contaminant and a drug allergen. It is a member of pyridines, a sulfonamide, a substituted aniline and a sulfonamide antibiotic. It derives from a sulfanilamide. it is a short-acting sulfonamide antibiotic and by-product of the non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug sulfasalazine. Its manufacture and use were discontinued in 1990.
