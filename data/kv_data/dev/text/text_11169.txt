A fungicide used to control Peronosporales on a range of crops including vines, hops and potatoes. It has a role as an environmental contaminant, a xenobiotic and an antifungal agrochemical. It is a member of ureas, a nitrile, an oxime O-ether and an aliphatic nitrogen antifungal agent.
