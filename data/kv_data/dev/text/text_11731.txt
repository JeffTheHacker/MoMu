It is an ether and a tertiary amino compound. it is a first generation antihistamine and ethanolamine with sedative and anti-allergic properties. it competitively inhibits the histamine-1 (H1) receptor, thereby alleviating the symptoms caused by endogenous histamine on bronchial, capillary and gastrointestinal smooth muscles. This prevents histamine-induced bronchoconstriction, vasodilation, increased capillary permeability, and GI smooth muscle spasms.
