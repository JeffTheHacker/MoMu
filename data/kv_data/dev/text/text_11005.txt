Ammonium citrate is a white granular solid. It is soluble in water. The primary hazard is the threat to the environment. Immediate steps should be taken to limit its spread to the environment. It is used in pharmaceuticals, and in chemical analysis. it is a citrate salt in which two of the three carboxy groups are deprotonated and associated with ammonium ions as counter-cations. It has a role as a buffer. It is an ammonium salt and a citrate salt.
