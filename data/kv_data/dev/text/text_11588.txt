3-(2, 3, 4-Trimethoxyphenyl)propanoic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as phenylpropanoic acids. Phenylpropanoic acids are compounds with a structure containing a benzene ring conjugated to a propanoic acid. 3-(2, 3, 4-Trimethoxyphenyl)propanoic acid is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
