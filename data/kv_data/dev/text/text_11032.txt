A post-emergence herbicide used (generally as a salt or ester) for the control of annual weeds in wheat and oilseed rape. It is not approved for use with the European Union. It has a role as a herbicide and a synthetic auxin. It is a member of benzothiazoles, a monocarboxylic acid and an organochlorine pesticide. It is a conjugate acid of a it(1-).
