it is a monocarboxylic acid amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group from 4,5-dimethyl-2-(trimethylsilyl)thiophene-3-carboxlic acid with the amino group of allylamine. A fungicide seed dressing for cereals used mainly to control 'take-all'. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is a member of thiophenes, an organosilicon compound, a monocarboxylic acid amide, an olefinic compound and an amide fungicide.
