A protease-activated receptor-1 antagonist used for the reduction of thrombotic cardiovascular events in patients with a history of myocardial infarction (MI) or with peripheral arterial disease. It has been shown to reduce the rate of a combined endpoint of cardiovascular death, MI, stroke and urgent coronary revascularisation. It has a role as a cardiovascular drug, a platelet aggregation inhibitor and a protease-activated receptor-1 antagonist. It contains a vorapaxar(1+).
