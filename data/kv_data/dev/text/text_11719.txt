3, 9-Dimethyluric acid, also known as 3, 9-dimethylate, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as xanthines. These are purine derivatives with a ketone group conjugated at carbons 2 and 6 of the purine moiety. 3, 9-Dimethyluric acid is soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). 3, 9-Dimethyluric acid has been primarily detected in urine.
