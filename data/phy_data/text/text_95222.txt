3-hydroxy-4-methylanthranilic acid is an aminobenzoic acid that is anthranilic acid substituted by a hydroxy group at position 3 and a methyl group at position 4. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a monohydroxybenzoic acid and an aminobenzoic acid. It derives from an anthranilic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-hydroxy-4-methylanthranilate.
