L-homocitrulline is a L-lysine derivative that is L-lysine having a carbamoyl group at the N(6)-position. It is found in individuals with urea cycle disorders. It has a role as a mouse metabolite and a human metabolite. It is a member of ureas, a L-lysine derivative and a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid. It is a tautomer of a L-homocitrulline zwitterion.
