2,2-dichloro-1-[5-(furan-2-yl)-2,2-dimethyl-1,3-oxazolidin-3-yl]ethanone is a member of the class of oxazolidines that is 2,2-dimethyl-1,3-oxazolidine which is substituted by a dichloroacetyl group at position 3 and by a furan-2-yl group at position 5. It is a member of furans, an organochlorine compound, a member of oxazolidines and a tertiary carboxamide.
