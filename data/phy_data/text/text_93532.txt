Pent-4-en-1-yl 2-[(2-furylmethyl)(imidazol-1-ylcarbonyl)amino]butanoate is a carboxylic ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 2-[(2-furylmethyl)(imidazol-1-ylcarbonyl)amino]butanoic acid with the hydroxy group of pent-4-en-1-ol. It is a N-acylimidazole, a member of furans, a carboxylic ester and an olefinic compound.
