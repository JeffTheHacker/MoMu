17beta-estradiol 3-sulfate is a steroid sulfate obtained by the formal condensation of sulfuric acid with the 3-hydroxy group of 17beta-estradiol. It has a role as a mammalian metabolite. It is a steroid sulfate and a 17beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a 17beta-estradiol. It is a conjugate acid of a 17beta-estradiol 3-sulfate(1-).
