Cilazapril is a pyridazinodiazepine resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of cilazaprilat with ethanol. It is a drug used in the treatment of hypertension and heart failure. It has a role as a prodrug, an antihypertensive agent and an EC 3.4.15.1 (peptidyl-dipeptidase A) inhibitor. It is an ethyl ester, a pyridazinodiazepine and a dicarboxylic acid monoester. It derives from a Cilazaprilat.
