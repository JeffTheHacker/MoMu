Lissamine flavine FF free acid is a member of the class of benzoisoquinolines that is 1,3-dioxo-2,3-dihydro-1H-benzo[de]isoquinoline-5-sulfonic acid substituted by 4-tolyl and amino groups at positions 2 and 6 respectively. The monosodium salt is the biological stain 'lissamine flavine FF'. It is a benzoisoquinoline, an arenesulfonic acid, an aromatic amine and a dicarboximide. It is a conjugate acid of a lissamine flavine FF(1-).
