2,5-bis(aziridin-1-yl)-1,4-benzoquinone is a member of the class of 1,4-benzoquinones that is p-benzoquinone in which the hydrogens at positions 2 and 5 are replaced by aziridin-1-yl groups. It has a role as a mutagen and an alkylating agent. It is a member of 1,4-benzoquinones and a member of aziridines.
