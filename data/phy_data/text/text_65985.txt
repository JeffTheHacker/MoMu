Iobitridol is a benzenedicarboxamide compound having N-substituted carbamoyl groups at the 1- and 3-positions, iodo substituents at the 2-, 4- and 6-positions and a 3-hydroxy-2-(hydroxymethyl)propanimido at position 5. It has a role as a xenobiotic, an environmental contaminant and a radioopaque medium. It is an organoiodine compound, a hexol and a benzenedicarboxamide.
