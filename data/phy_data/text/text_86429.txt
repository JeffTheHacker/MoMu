Flufenacet is an aromatic amide that is acetamide in which the amino hydrogens have been replaced by a propan-2-yl and 4-fluorophenyl groups while the methyl hydrogen is replaced by a [5-(trifluoromethyl)-1,3,4-thiadiazol-2-yl]oxy group. It has a role as an environmental contaminant, a xenobiotic and a herbicide. It is a member of monofluorobenzenes, an aromatic amide and a member of thiadiazoles.
