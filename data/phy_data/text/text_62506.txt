Sulcofuron is an organochlorine pesticide consisting of 1,3-diphenylurea having chloro substituents at the 3-, 4- and 5'-positions and a 4-chloro-2-sulfophenoxy group at the 2'-position. It has a role as an epitope and an insecticide. It is an organochlorine pesticide, an arenesulfonic acid, a dichlorobenzene and a member of phenylureas. It derives from a 1,3-diphenylurea. It is a conjugate acid of a sulcofuronate.
