2-methylpropane-1,2-diol is a glycol that is 2-methylpropane in which the two hydroxy groups are located at positions 1 and 2.. It has a role as a mouse metabolite, a rat metabolite, a human urinary metabolite, a human xenobiotic metabolite and a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite. It is a primary alcohol, a glycol and a tertiary alcohol. It derives from a hydride of an isobutane.
