Acibenzolar-S-methyl is a benzothiadiazole that is the S-methyl thioester derivative of acibenzolar. A profungicide (by hydrolysis of the thioester group to give the corresponding carboxylic acid), it is used as a fungicide and plant activator on a variety of crops, including cotton, chili peppers, lettuce, onions, spinach, tobacco, and tomatoes. It has a role as a plant activator, an antifungal agrochemical and a profungicide. It is a benzothiadiazole and a thioester. It derives from an acibenzolar.
