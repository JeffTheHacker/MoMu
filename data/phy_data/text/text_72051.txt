1-(2,4-dichloro-10,11-dihydrodibenzo[a,d][7]annulen-5-yl)imidazole is a member of the class of dibenzannulenes that is 10,11-dihydrodibenzo[a,d][7]annulene carrying two chloro substituents at positions 2 and 4 as well as an imidazol-1-yl substituent at position 5. It is a member of imidazoles, an organochlorine compound and a dibenzannulene. It derives from a hydride of a dibenzo[a,d][7]annulene.
