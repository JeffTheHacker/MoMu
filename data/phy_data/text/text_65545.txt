16alpha-hydroxytestosterone is a C19-steroid that is testosterone in which the hydrogen at the 16alpha position has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It has a role as an androgen. It is a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a 16alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, a C19-steroid, an androstanoid, a diol and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a testosterone.
