D617 is a nitrile that is pentanenitrile substituted by a 3,4-dimethoxyphenyl group at position 2, a methylamino group at position 4 and an isopropyl group at position 2. It is a metabolite of the drug verapamil. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite and a drug metabolite. It is a dimethoxybenzene, a nitrile and a secondary amino compound.
