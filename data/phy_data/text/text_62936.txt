Atenolol acid is a monocarboxylic acid that is phenylacetic acid substituted by a 2-hydroxy-3-(propan-2-ylamino)propoxy group at position 4. It is a metabolite of the drug atenolol. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite and a drug metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid, an aromatic ether, a secondary amino compound and a secondary alcohol.
