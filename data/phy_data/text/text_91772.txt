Tribenuron is an N-sulfonylurea that is N-(2-carboxybenzenesulfonyl)urea bearing additional methyl and 4-methoxy-6-methyl-1,3,5-triazin-2-yl substituents at position N3. A foliar acting, post-emergence herbicide used to control broad-leaved weeds in cereals, normally used as the methyl variant. It has a role as a herbicide. It is a N-sulfonylurea, a methoxy-1,3,5-triazine and a member of benzoic acids.
