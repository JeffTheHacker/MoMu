N-2-fluorenylphthalamic acid is a member of the class of fluorenes that is 9H-fluorene substituted by a (2-carboxybenzoyl)amino group at position 2. It has a role as a carcinogenic agent. It is a member of fluorenes, a member of benzoic acids, a member of benzamides, a secondary carboxamide and a dicarboxylic acid monoamide.
