2-methoxy-17beta-estradiol is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, being 17beta-estradiol methoxylated at C-2. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an antimitotic, a metabolite, a human metabolite, a mouse metabolite and an angiogenesis modulating agent. It is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid and a 3-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a 17beta-estradiol.
