4-hydroxy-8-methoxyquinaldic acid is a quinolinemonocarboxylic acid that is kynurenic acid which is substituted by a methoxy group at position 8. It has a role as a metabolite, a carcinogenic agent and a mouse metabolite. It is a quinolinemonocarboxylic acid, a monohydroxyquinoline and an aromatic ether. It derives from a kynurenic acid.
