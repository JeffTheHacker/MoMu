Tubulosine is a member of the class of beta-carbolines that is tubulosan bearing methoxy groups at positions 10 and 11 and a hydroxy group at the 8' position. It is an isoquinoline alkaloid, a secondary amino compound, a tertiary amino compound, a member of beta-carbolines, a member of isoquinolines and a member of phenols. It derives from a hydride of a tubulosan.
