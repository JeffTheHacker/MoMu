1-methyl-1-[1-oxo-1-(10H-phenothiazin-10-yl)propan-2-yl]pyrrolidinium is a member of the class of phenothiazines that is 10H-phenothiazine which is substituted by a 2-(1-methylpyrrolidin-1-ium-1-yl)propanoyl group at position 10. It is a member of phenothiazines, a tertiary carboxamide and a quaternary ammonium ion.
