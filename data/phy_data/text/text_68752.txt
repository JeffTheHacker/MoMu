Miroprofen is a monocarboxylic acid that is propionic acid in which one of the hydrogens at position 2 is substituted by a 4-(imidazo[1,2-a]pyridin-2-yl)phenyl group. A non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug that also exhibits analgesic, antipyretic and platelet aggregation inhibition properties. It has a role as a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug, an antipyretic, a non-narcotic analgesic and a platelet aggregation inhibitor. It is an imidazopyridine and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a propionic acid.
