Sulcotrione is an aromatic ketone that is cyclohexane-1,3-dione substituted by a 2-chloro-4-(methylsulfonyl)benzoyl group at position 2. It has a role as an environmental contaminant, a xenobiotic, a herbicide and a carotenoid biosynthesis inhibitor. It is an aromatic ketone, a sulfone, a member of cyclohexanones and a beta-triketone.
