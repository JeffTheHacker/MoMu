Pifarnine is a member of the class of benzodioxoles that is 1,3-benzodioxole substituted by a [4-(3,7,11-trimethyldodeca-2,6,10-trien-1-yl)piperazin-1-yl]methyl group at position 5. It is an anti-ulcer agent. It has a role as an anti-ulcer drug. It is a member of benzodioxoles, a N-alkylpiperazine and an olefinic compound.
