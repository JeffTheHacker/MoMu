Cefazedone is a first-generation cephalosporin antibiotic having [(5-methyl-1,3,4-thiadiazol-2-yl)sulfanyl]methyl and [(3,5-dichloro-4-oxopyridin-1(4H)-yl)acetamido side-groups located at positions 3 and 7 respectively. It has a role as an antibacterial drug. It is a cephalosporin, a semisynthetic derivative, a member of thiadiazoles, a carboxylic acid and a member of 4-pyridones.
