3-methylflavone-8-carboxylic acid is a member of the class of flavones that is flavone substituted at position 3 by a methyl group and at position 8 by a carboxylic acid group. It has a role as an EC 3.1.4.* (phosphoric diester hydrolase) inhibitor. It is a member of flavones and an oxo monocarboxylic acid.
