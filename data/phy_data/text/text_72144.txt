Xibornol is a bridged compound that is 3,4-xylenol carrying an additional isobornyl substituent at position 6. A lipophilic antibacterial drug mainly used in spray dosage forms for the local treatment of infection and inflammation of the throat. It has a role as an antibacterial drug. It is a member of phenols and a bridged compound. It derives from a hydride of a bornane.
