Myrtenic acid is a monoterpenoid that is 6,6-dimethylbicyclo[3.1.1]hept-2-ene substituted by a carboxy group at position 2. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite, a human urinary metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a monoterpenoid, an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid and a bridged compound. It derives from an alpha-pinene.
