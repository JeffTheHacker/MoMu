Sophoraflavanone G is a tetrahydroxyflavanone having a structure of naringenin bearing an additional hydroxyl substituent at position 2' as well as a (2R)-5-methyl-2-(prop-1-en-2-yl)hex-4-en-1-yl (lavandulyl) substituent at position 8'. It has a role as an antioxidant, an antimalarial, an antimicrobial agent and a plant metabolite. It is a tetrahydroxyflavanone, a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones and a (2S)-flavan-4-one. It derives from a (S)-naringenin.
