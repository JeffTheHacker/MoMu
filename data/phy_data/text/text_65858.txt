Egualen is a member of the class of azulenes carrying sulfo, ethyl and isopropyl substituents at positions 1, 3 and 7 respectively. Used (in the form of its sodium salt) for treatment of gastric ulcers. It has a role as an anti-ulcer drug and a thromboxane A2 antagonist. It is an arenesulfonic acid and a member of azulenes. It is a conjugate acid of an egualen(1-).
