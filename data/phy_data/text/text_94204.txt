24-methylenecycloartanol is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is (9beta)-24-methylene-9,19-cyclolanostane which carries a hydroxy group at position 3beta. It is isolated from several plant species including Euphorbia, Epidendrum, Psychotria and Sideritis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a 3beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a hydride of a cycloartane.
