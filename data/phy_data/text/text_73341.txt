Amprolium is an organic chloride salt having 1-[(4-amino-2-propylpyrimidin-5-yl)methyl]-2-methylpyridin-1-ium as the counterion. Used for prevention of coccidiosis in poultry and cattle. It has a role as a coccidiostat. It is a pyridinium salt and an organic chloride salt. It contains an amprolium(1+).
