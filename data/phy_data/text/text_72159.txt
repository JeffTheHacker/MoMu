Tiquizium bromide is a organic bromide salt of tiquizium. It is an antispasmodic drug used for the treatment of convulsion and hypermobility in gastritis, gastric ulcer, duodenal ulcer, enteritis, irritable bowel syndrome, gallbladder disease, biliary tract disease and urolithiasis. It has a role as a muscarinic antagonist, an antispasmodic drug and an anti-ulcer drug. It is an organic bromide salt and a quaternary ammonium salt. It contains a tiquizium.
