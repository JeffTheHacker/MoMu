1-methyl-4-imidazoleacetic acid is a monocarboxylic acid that is acetic acid in which one of the methyl hydrogens has been replaced by a 1-methyl-1H-imidazol-4-yl group. It has a role as a metabolite and a GABA agonist. It is a member of imidazoles and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from an acetic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-methyl-4-imidazoleacetate.
