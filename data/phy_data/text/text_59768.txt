Methyl 3-{4-[2-hydroxy-3-(propan-2-ylamino)propoxy]phenyl}propanoate is a methyl ester that is methyl 3-(4-hydroxyphenyl)propanoate in which the hydrogen attached to the phenolic hydroxy group is substituted by a 2-hydroxy-3-(isopropylamino)propyl group. It is an aromatic ether, a member of ethanolamines, a methyl ester, a secondary alcohol and a secondary amino compound. It derives from a 3-{4-[2-hydroxy-3-(propan-2-ylamino)propoxy]phenyl}propanoic acid.
