2-(4-Hydroxy-3, 5-dimethoxyphenyl)acetic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as methoxyphenols. Methoxyphenols are compounds containing a methoxy group attached to the benzene ring of a phenol moiety. 2-(4-Hydroxy-3, 5-dimethoxyphenyl)acetic acid is slightly soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
