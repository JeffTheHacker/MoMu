N-it is a sulfonamide compound having a 4-acetamidophenyl group attached to the sulfur atom and a 1,2-oxazol-3-yl group attached to the nitrogen atom. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite and a drug metabolite. It is a member of isoxazoles and a sulfonamide. It derives from a sulfamethoxazole and a sulfanilamide.
