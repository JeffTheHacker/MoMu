3-[4-Hydroxy-3-(3-methylbut-2-en-1-yl)phenyl]prop-2-enoic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxycinnamic acids. Hydroxycinnamic acids are compounds containing an cinnamic acid where the benzene ring is hydroxylated. 3-[4-Hydroxy-3-(3-methylbut-2-en-1-yl)phenyl]prop-2-enoic acid is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
