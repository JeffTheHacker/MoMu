N-(indole-3-acetyl)-L-aspartic acid is an N-acyl-L-aspartic acid in which the acyl group is specified as indole-3-acetyl. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an indole-L-aspartic acid conjugate, an indoleacetic acid amide conjugate and a N-acyl-L-aspartic acid. It derives from an indole-3-acetic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-(indole-3-acetyl)-L-aspartate(2-).
