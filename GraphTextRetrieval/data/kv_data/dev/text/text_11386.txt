2, 3-Diethyl-5, 6-dimethylpyrazine belongs to the class of organic compounds known as pyrazines. Pyrazines are compounds containing a pyrazine ring, which is a six-member aromatic heterocycle, that consists of two nitrogen atoms (at positions 1 and 4) and four carbon atoms. 2, 3-Diethyl-5, 6-dimethylpyrazine is slightly soluble (in water) and a moderately basic compound (based on its pKa). Outside of the human body, 2, 3-diethyl-5, 6-dimethylpyrazine can be found in coffee and coffee products. This makes 2, 3-diethyl-5, 6-dimethylpyrazine a potential biomarker for the consumption of this food product.
