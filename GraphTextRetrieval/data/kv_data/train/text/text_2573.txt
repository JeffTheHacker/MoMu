(NTP, 1992) Diethyl 2-[(dimethoxyphosphoryl)thio]succinate is a diester that is diethyl succinate in which position 2 is substituted by a (dimethoxyphosphoryl)thio group. It is a diester, an ethyl ester and an organic thiophosphate.
