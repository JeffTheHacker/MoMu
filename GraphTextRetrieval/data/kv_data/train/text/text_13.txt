Magnoshinin is a neolignan that consists of 1,2-dihydronaphthalene substituted by a 2,4,5-trimethoxyphenyl group at position 1 (the 1S,2R stereoisomer), methyl groups at positions 2 and 3, and methoxy groups at positions 5, 7 and 8 repectively. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent and a metabolite. It is a member of methoxybenzenes, a member of naphthalenes and a neolignan.
