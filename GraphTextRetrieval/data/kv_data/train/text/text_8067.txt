1, N2-Propanodeoxyguanosine belongs to the class of organic compounds known as nucleoside and nucleotide analogues. These are analogues of nucleosides and nucleotides. These include phosphonated nucleosides, C-glycosylated nucleoside bases, analogues where the sugar unit is a pyranose, and carbocyclic nucleosides, among others. 1, N2-Propanodeoxyguanosine is soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
