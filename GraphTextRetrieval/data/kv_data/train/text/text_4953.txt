it is a 5-methyl-5-(4-phenoxyphenyl)-3-(phenylamino)-1,3-oxazolidine-2,4-dione that is the (active) (S)-enantiomer of famoxadone. It prevents spore germination and mycelial growth of sensitive fungi and is used in agriculture for the control of various fungal diseases. It has a role as a fungicide, an agrochemical, a mitochondrial cytochrome-bc1 complex inhibitor and a quinone outside inhibitor. It is an enantiomer of a (R)-famoxadone.
