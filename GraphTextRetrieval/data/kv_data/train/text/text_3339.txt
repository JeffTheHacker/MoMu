Mobile liquid; faint odor of herring. Used as a drug for the treatment of cancer. Formerly used as a gas warfare agent. it is a it and an organochlorine compound. It has a role as an alkylating agent. it is a synthetic agent related to sulphur mustard with antineoplastic and immunosuppressive properties. it (a member of a family of chemotherapy agents including cyclophosphamide and chlorambucil) is an irritant and carcinogenic agent metabolized to a highly reactive ethylene immonium derivative; the ethylene immonium derivative alkylates DNA and inhibits DNA replication.
