1-(2-Hydroxy-4, 6-dimethoxyphenyl)-3-phenylprop-2-en-1-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 2'-hydroxychalcones. These are organic compounds containing chalcone skeleton that carries a hydroxyl group at the 2'-position. 1-(2-Hydroxy-4, 6-dimethoxyphenyl)-3-phenylprop-2-en-1-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
