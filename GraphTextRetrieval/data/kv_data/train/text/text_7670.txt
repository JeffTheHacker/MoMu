1-O-caffeoylquinic acid is an alkyl caffeate ester obtained by the formal condensation of the carboxy group of trans-caffeic acid with the 1-hydroxy group of (-)-quinic acid. It has a role as a Camellia sinensis metabolite, a NF-kappaB inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and an antioxidant. It is a quinic acid and an alkyl caffeate ester. It derives from a trans-caffeic acid. It derives from a hydride of a (-)-quinic acid.
