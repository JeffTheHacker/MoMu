3-Acetyl-2, 5-dimethylthiophene, also known as 1-(2, 5-dimethyl-3-thienyl)-ethanone or 2, 5-dimethyl-3-acetylthiophene, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as aryl alkyl ketones. These are ketones have the generic structure RC(=O)R', where R = aryl group and R'=alkyl group. 3-Acetyl-2, 5-dimethylthiophene is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, 3-acetyl-2, 5-dimethylthiophene is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 3-Acetyl-2, 5-dimethylthiophene has a burnt, meaty, and roasted taste.
