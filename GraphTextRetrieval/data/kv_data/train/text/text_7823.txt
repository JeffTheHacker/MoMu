SU6656 is a member of the class of oxindoles that is 3-methyleneoxindole in which the hydrogeh at position 5 has been replaced by a dimethylaminosulfonyl group and in which one of the hydrogens of the methylene group has been replaced by a 4,5,6,7-tetrahydro-indol-2-yl group. It is a specific inhibitor of Src family kinase. It has a role as an EC 2.7.10.2 (non-specific protein-tyrosine kinase) inhibitor, an Aurora kinase inhibitor and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of oxindoles and a sulfonamide.
