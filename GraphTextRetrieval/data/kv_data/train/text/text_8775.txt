It is isolated from the fermentation extracts of Sporormiella australis and exhibits antifungal activity by interfering with the fungal lipid metabolism. It has a role as a metabolite, an EC 2.3.1.24 (sphingosine N-acyltransferase) inhibitor, a HIV-1 integrase inhibitor and an antifungal agent. It is an enol, an enone, a secondary alcohol and a carbobicyclic compound.
