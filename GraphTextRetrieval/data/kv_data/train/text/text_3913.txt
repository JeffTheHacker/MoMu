N(4)-it is a sulfonamide that is benzenesulfonamide substituted by an acetylamino group at position 4 and a 1,3-thiazol-2-yl group at the nitrogen atom. It is a metabolite of sulfathiazole. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a sulfonamide, a member of acetamides and a member of 1,3-thiazoles.
