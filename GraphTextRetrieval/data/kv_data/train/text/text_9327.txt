1, 5-Hexanediol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as fatty alcohols. These are aliphatic alcohols consisting of a chain of a least six carbon atoms. Thus, 1, 5-hexanediol is considered to be a fatty alcohol lipid molecule. 1, 5-Hexanediol is soluble (in water) and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). 1, 5-Hexanediol has been primarily detected in urine. Within the cell, 1, 5-hexanediol is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
