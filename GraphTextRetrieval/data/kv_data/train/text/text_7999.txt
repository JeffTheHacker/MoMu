Pure white crystalline solid. Used as a fungicide. Irritates skin and mucous membranes. (2-chlorophenyl)(4-chlorophenyl)pyit-5-ylmethanol is a member of the class of pyites that is pyit-5-ylmethanol in which one of the hydrogens attached to the carbon bearing the hydroxy group is replaced by a 2-chlorophenyl group while the other is replaced by a 4-chlorophenyl group. It is a tertiary alcohol, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a member of pyites.
