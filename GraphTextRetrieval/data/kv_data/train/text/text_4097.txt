Hyodeoxycholic acid 6-O-(beta-D-glucuronide) is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is hyodeoxycholic acid having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 6. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, a dicarboxylic acid and a steroid glucosiduronic acid. It derives from a hyodeoxycholic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a hyodeoxycholate 6-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(2-).
