3-Hexenal, also known as fema 3923 or hex-3-enal, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as medium-chain aldehydes. These are an aldehyde with a chain length containing between 6 and 12 carbon atoms. 3-Hexenal exists as a solid, slightly soluble (in water), and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 3-hexenal is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 3-Hexenal has an apple, green, and leafy taste.
