1, 2, 3, 4-Tetrahydro-6-propanoylpyridine, also known as it or PTP, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as tetrahydropyridines. These are derivatives of pyridine in which two double bonds in the pyridine moiety are reduced by adding four hydrogen atoms. 1, 2, 3, 4-Tetrahydro-6-propanoylpyridine is soluble (in water) and a strong basic compound (based on its pKa).
