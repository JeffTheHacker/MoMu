(5Z,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid is a DHET obtained by formal dihydroxylation across the 8,9-double bond of arachidonic acid. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is a DHET, a secondary allylic alcohol and a diol. It is a conjugate acid of a (5Z,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoate.
