(R)-it is a fourteen-membered macrocyclic diterpene consisting of 1,5,9-trimethyl-12-(prop-1-en-2-yl)cyclotetradecane having three endocyclic double bonds located at positions 1, 5 and 9. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite, a coral metabolite and an animal metabolite. It is a cycloalkatriene, a diterpene and a macrocycle. It derives from a hydride of a cembrane.
