Xi-2, 5-dihydro-2, 4-dimethylthiazole belongs to the class of organic compounds known as thiazolines. These are heterocyclic compounds containing a five-member unsaturated aliphatic ring with one nitrogen atom, one sulfur atom, three carbon atoms. Xi-2, 5-dihydro-2, 4-dimethylthiazole is slightly soluble (in water) and a very strong basic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, xi-2, 5-dihydro-2, 4-dimethylthiazole is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Outside of the human body, xi-2, 5-dihydro-2, 4-dimethylthiazole can be found in nuts. This makes xi-2, 5-dihydro-2, 4-dimethylthiazole a potential biomarker for the consumption of this food product.
