Ac-Tyr-Val-Ala-Asp-7-amino-4-trifluoromethylcoumarin is a tetrapeptide obtained by formal condensation of the C-terminal carboxy group of Ac-Tyr-Val-Ala-Asp with the amino group of 7-amino-4-trifluoromethylcoumarin. It has a role as a protease inhibitor. It is a tetrapeptide, a member of acetamides, a member of coumarins and an organofluorine compound. It derives from a 7-amino-4-(trifluoromethyl)coumarin.
