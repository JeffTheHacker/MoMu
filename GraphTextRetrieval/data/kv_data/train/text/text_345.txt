CDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-glucose is a CDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-D-glucose in which the anomeric centre of the deoxyglucose fragment has alpha-configuration. It is a CDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-D-glucose and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It is a conjugate acid of a CDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-glucose(2-).
