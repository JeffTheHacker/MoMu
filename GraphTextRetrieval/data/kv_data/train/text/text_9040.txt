2-Furanylmethyl butanoate, also known as 2-it or it, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as fatty acid esters. These are carboxylic ester derivatives of a fatty acid. 2-Furanylmethyl butanoate is slightly soluble (in water) and an extremely weak basic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). 2-Furanylmethyl butanoate has been primarily detected in urine. Within the cell, 2-furanylmethyl butanoate is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2-Furanylmethyl butanoate has a fruity, grape, and green taste.
