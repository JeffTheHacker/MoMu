3-Acetyl-2, 5-dimethylfuran, also known as 2, 5-dimethyl-3-acetylfuran or fema 3391, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as aryl alkyl ketones. These are ketones have the generic structure RC(=O)R', where R = aryl group and R'=alkyl group. 3-Acetyl-2, 5-dimethylfuran is slightly soluble (in water) and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 3-acetyl-2, 5-dimethylfuran is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 3-Acetyl-2, 5-dimethylfuran has a sweet, cocoa, and corn taste.
