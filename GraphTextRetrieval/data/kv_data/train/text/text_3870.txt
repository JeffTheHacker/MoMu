enol-3-Ethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione, also known as it or 2-cyclopenten-1-one, 2-hydroxy-3-ethyl, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as cyclic ketones. These are organic compounds containing a ketone that is conjugated to a cyclic moiety. enol-3-Ethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione exists as a solid, soluble (in water), and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, enol-3-ethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione is primarily located in the cytoplasm. enol-3-Ethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione has a sweet, butterscotch, and caramel taste.
