L-rhamnosyl-3-hydroxydecanoyl-3-hydroxydecanoic acid is a glycolipid, an alpha-L-rhamnoside of 3-[(3-hydroxydecanoyl)oxy]decanoic acid. It has a role as a nonionic surfactant. It is an alpha-L-rhamnoside and a glycolipid. It is a conjugate acid of a L-rhamnosyl-3-hydroxydecanoyl-3-hydroxydecanoate.
