1H-Pyrrolo[2, 1-c][1, 4]thiazine belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 1, 4-thiazines. These are organic compounds containing 1, 4-thiazine, a six-member ring with a nitrogen and a sulfur atoms in ring positions 1 and 4 respectively, as well as two double bonds. Within the cell, 1H-pyrrolo[2, 1-c][1, 4]thiazine is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
