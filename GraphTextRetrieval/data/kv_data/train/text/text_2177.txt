2-Benzyl-4, 5-dimethyl-1, 3-dioxolane, also known as acido tiaprofenico or fema 2875, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as benzene and substituted derivatives. These are aromatic compounds containing one monocyclic ring system consisting of benzene. 2-Benzyl-4, 5-dimethyl-1, 3-dioxolane is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, 2-benzyl-4, 5-dimethyl-1, 3-dioxolane is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2-Benzyl-4, 5-dimethyl-1, 3-dioxolane has a sweet, earthy, and floral taste.
