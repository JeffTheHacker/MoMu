Trinitrobenzene sulfonic acid appears as a detonating explosive substance derived from an organic sulfonic acid. Primary hazard is blast of an instantaneous explosion, not flying projectiles or fragments. Under prolonged exposure to fire or heat the containers may explode violently. it is the arenesulfonic acid that is benzenesulfonic acid with three nitro substituents in the 2-, 4- and 6-positions. It has a role as an epitope, an explosive and a reagent. It is a C-nitro compound and an arenesulfonic acid.
