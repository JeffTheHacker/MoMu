3, 3-Dichloro-4-(dichloromethylene)-2, 5-pyrrolidinedione, also known as dc-dcm-P, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as pyrrolidine-2-ones. These are pyrrolidines which bear a C=O group at position 2 of the pyrrolidine ring. 3, 3-Dichloro-4-(dichloromethylene)-2, 5-pyrrolidinedione is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, 3, 3-dichloro-4-(dichloromethylene)-2, 5-pyrrolidinedione is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
