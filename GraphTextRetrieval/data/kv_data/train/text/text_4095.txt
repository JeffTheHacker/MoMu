N-SulfO-D-glucosamine, also known as N-sulpho-D-glucosamine, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hexoses. These are monosaccharides in which the sugar unit is a is a six-carbon containing moeity. N-SulfO-D-glucosamine is soluble (in water) and an extremely strong acidic compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, N-sulfO-D-glucosamine is primarily located in the cytoplasm.
