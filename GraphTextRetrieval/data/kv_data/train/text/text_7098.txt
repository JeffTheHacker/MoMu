N-(4-Aminobutyl)-3-(3, 4-dihydroxyphenyl)propanimidic acid belongs to the class of organic compounds known as catechols. Catechols are compounds containing a 1, 2-benzenediol moiety. N-(4-Aminobutyl)-3-(3, 4-dihydroxyphenyl)propanimidic acid is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
