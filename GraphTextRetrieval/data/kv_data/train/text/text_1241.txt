11-nor-9-carboxy-Delta(9)-tetrahydrocannabinol is a phytocannabinoid that is Delta(9)-tetrahydrocannabinol in which the C-11 methyl has been fully oxidised to a carboxy group. Further enzymatic oxidation product of 11-hydroxy-Delta(9)-tetrahydrocannabinol. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It derives from a Delta(9)-tetrahydrocannabinol.
