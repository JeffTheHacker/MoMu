Quizalofop-P-tefuryl is a tetrahydrofurylmethyl ester resulting from the formyl condensation of the carboxy group of quizalofop-P with the hydroxy group of tetrahydrofurfuryl alcohol. It has a role as a proherbicide and an agrochemical. It is a quinoxaline herbicide, a tetrahydrofurylmethyl ester, an aromatic ether and an organochlorine compound. It derives from a quizalofop-P and a tetrahydrofurfuryl alcohol.
