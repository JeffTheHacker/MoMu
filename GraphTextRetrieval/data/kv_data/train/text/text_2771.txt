Thymidine 3'-monophosphate belongs to the class of organic compounds known as ribonucleoside 3'-phosphates. These are ribonucleosides that contain a phosphate group attached to the C-3 carbon of the ribose or deoxyribose moiety. The nucleobases here are limited to purine, pyrimidine, and pyridine derivatives. Thymidine 3'-monophosphate is soluble (in water) and a moderately acidic compound (based on its pKa).
