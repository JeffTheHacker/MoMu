A fungicide for cereals, beet and brassicas used to control a range of diseases including powdery mildew, rusts, bunts and smuts. It has a role as an EC 1.14.13.70 (sterol 14alpha-demethylase) inhibitor, a xenobiotic metabolite and an antifungal agrochemical. It is an aromatic ether, a member of monochlorobenzenes, a conazole fungicide, a triazole fungicide, a secondary alcohol and a hemiaminal ether.
