5-O-phosphono-alpha-D-ribofuranosyl diphosphate is a derivative of alpha-D-ribose having a phosphate group at the 5-position and a diphosphate at the 1-position. It has a role as a human metabolite, a plant metabolite, an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It derives from an alpha-D-ribose. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-O-phosphonato-alpha-D-ribofuranosyl diphosphate(5-).
