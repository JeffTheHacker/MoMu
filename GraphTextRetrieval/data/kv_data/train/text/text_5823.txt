4-(4-Hydroxyphenyl)but-3-en-2-one, also known as 4-hydroxybenzalacetone or 4-hydroxycinnamoylmethane, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as hydroxycinnamic acids and derivatives. Hydroxycinnamic acids and derivatives are compounds containing an cinnamic acid (or a derivative thereof) where the benzene ring is hydroxylated. 4-(4-Hydroxyphenyl)but-3-en-2-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
