Ethyl 3-[(2-furanylmethyl)thio]propanoate, also known as it or fema 3674, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as heteroaromatic compounds. Heteroaromatic compounds are compounds containing an aromatic ring where a carbon atom is linked to an hetero atom. Ethyl 3-[(2-furanylmethyl)thio]propanoate is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, ethyl 3-[(2-furanylmethyl)thio]propanoate is primarily located in the cytoplasm. Ethyl 3-[(2-furanylmethyl)thio]propanoate has an animal, coffee, and garlic taste.
