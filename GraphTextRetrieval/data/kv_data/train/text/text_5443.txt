6-Hydroxy-2-(3, 4, 5-trimethoxyphenyl)-4H-chromen-4-one belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 4'-o-methylated flavonoids. These are flavonoids with methoxy groups attached to the C4' atom of the flavonoid backbone. 6-Hydroxy-2-(3, 4, 5-trimethoxyphenyl)-4H-chromen-4-one is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
