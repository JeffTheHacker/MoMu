it is a hydroxy-amino acid consisting of L-threonine having a hydroxy substituent at the 4-position. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite and an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a L-threonine derivative, a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid and a hydroxy-amino acid. It is a tautomer of a it zwitterion.
