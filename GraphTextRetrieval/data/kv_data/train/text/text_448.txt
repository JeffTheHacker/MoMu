ADP-5-ethyl-4-methylthiazole-2-carboxylic acid is an 1,3-thiazolemonocarboxylic acid that is ADP in which one of the diphosphate hydrogens has been replaced by a 2-(2-carboxy-4-methyl-1,3-thiazol-5-yl)ethyl group. It derives from an ADP. It is a conjugate acid of an ADP-5-ethyl-4-methylthiazole-2-carboxylate(3-).
