5-[(9Z)-octadecenoyloxy]octadecanoic acid is a fatty acid ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of (9Z)-octadecenoic acid with the hydroxy group of 5-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a fatty acid ester and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a 5-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid and an oleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-[(9Z)-octadecenoyloxy]octadecanoate.
