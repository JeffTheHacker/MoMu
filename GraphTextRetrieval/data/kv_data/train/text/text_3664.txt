3-(4-Methoxyphenyl)prop-2-en-1-ol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as cinnamyl alcohols. These are aromatic alcohols containing a 3-phenylprop-2-en-1-ol moiety. 3-(4-Methoxyphenyl)prop-2-en-1-ol is slightly soluble (in water) and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa).
