Guanosine-5', 3'-tetraphosphate, also known as tetraphosphate, guanosine, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as purine ribonucleoside diphosphates. These are purine ribobucleotides with diphosphate group linked to the ribose moiety. Guanosine-5', 3'-tetraphosphate is soluble (in water) and a moderately acidic compound (based on its pKa).
