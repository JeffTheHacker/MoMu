Used to manufacture other chemicals, in dyeing, in printing, in fungicides, as a wood preservative. it is an inorganic chloride of copper in which the metal is in the +2 oxidation state. It has a role as an EC 5.3.3.5 (cholestenol Delta-isomerase) inhibitor. It is an inorganic chloride and a copper molecular entity. It contains a copper(2+).
