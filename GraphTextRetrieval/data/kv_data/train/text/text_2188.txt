Barbinine is a diterpene alkaloid that is a phytotoxin produced by several Delphinium species. It has a role as a phytotoxin. It is a pyrrolidinone, a dicarboximide, a diterpene alkaloid, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, an ether, a tertiary alcohol, a tertiary amino compound and a benzoate ester. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
