N, N'-it, also known as carcinogen or DNPZ, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as piperazines. Piperazines are compounds containing a piperazine ring, which is a saturated aliphatic six-member heterocyclic with two nitrogen atoms at positions 1 and 4, as well as four carbon atoms. N, N'-it exists as a solid, soluble (in water), and a strong basic compound (based on its pKa).
