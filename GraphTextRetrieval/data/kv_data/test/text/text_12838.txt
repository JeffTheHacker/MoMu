4-ethylamino-6-isopropylamino-1,3,5-triazin-2-ol is a monohydroxy-1,3,5-triazine that is atrazine in which the chloro group has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a diamino-1,3,5-triazine and a monohydroxy-1,3,5-triazine. It derives from an atrazine.
