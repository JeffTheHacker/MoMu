N, N, N-Trimethylethenaminium belongs to the class of organic compounds known as quaternary ammonium salts. Quaternary ammonium salts are compounds containing positively charged polyatomic ion of the structure NR4+, R being an alkyl group or an aryl group. N, N, N-Trimethylethenaminium is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
