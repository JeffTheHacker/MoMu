it is a member of the class of piperidines that is 3-(piperidin-1-yl)propan-1-ol in which one of the hydrogen atoms at the 1-position is substituted by cyclopentyl, and the other is substituted by phenyl. A central anticholinergic, it is used as its hydrochloride salt in the management and treatment of Parkinson's disease. It has a role as an antiparkinson drug, a muscarinic antagonist and an antidyskinesia agent. It is a tertiary alcohol, a member of piperidines and a tertiary amino compound.
