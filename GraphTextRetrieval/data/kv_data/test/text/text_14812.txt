it is an amidobenzoic acid obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of (5,5,8,8-tetramethyl-5,6,7,8-tetrahydronaphthalen-2-yl)benzoic acid with the anilino group of 4-aminobenzoic acid. A selective RARalpha agonist. It has a role as a retinoic acid receptor alpha/beta agonist and an antineoplastic agent. It is an amidobenzoic acid and a member of tetralins.
