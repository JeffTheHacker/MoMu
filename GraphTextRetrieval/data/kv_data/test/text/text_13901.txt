In particular, 4-(3-hydroxyprop-1-en-1-yl)phenol can be biosynthesized from cinnamyl alcohol. 4-(3-Hydroxyprop-1-en-1-yl)phenol can also be converted into 4-hydroxycinnamyl alcohol 4-beta-D-glucoside.
