N-(indole-3-acetyl)phenylalanine is an indoleacetic acid amide conjugate obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of indole-3-acetic acid with the amino group of phenylalanine. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an indoleacetic acid amide conjugate and a phenylalanine derivative. It derives from an indole-3-acetic acid.
