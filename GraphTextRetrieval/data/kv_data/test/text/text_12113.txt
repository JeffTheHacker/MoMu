5-Keto-D-gluconate, also known as 5-dehydrogluconate or D-tagaturonate, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as medium-chain hydroxy acids and derivatives. These are hydroxy acids with a 6 to 12 carbon atoms long side chain. 5-Keto-D-gluconate is soluble (in water) and a weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa). 5-Keto-D-gluconate has been primarily detected in feces.
