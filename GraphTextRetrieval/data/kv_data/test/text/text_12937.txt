Beta-D-fructofuranose 2,6-bisphosphate is a D-fructofuranose 2,6-bisphosphate with a beta-configuration at the anomeric centre. It has a role as a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It derives from a beta-D-fructofuranose. It is a conjugate acid of a beta-D-fructofuranose 2,6-bisphosphate(4-).
