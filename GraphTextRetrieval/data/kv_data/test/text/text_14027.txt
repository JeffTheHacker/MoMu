These are organic compounds containing a ketone that is conjugated to a cyclic moiety. 3, 5-Dimethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione exists as a solid, soluble (in water), and an extremely weak acidic (essentially neutral) compound (based on its pKa). Within the cell, 3, 5-dimethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 3, 5-Dimethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione is a sweet, caramel, and maple tasting compound that can be found in coffee and coffee products. This makes 3, 5-dimethyl-1, 2-cyclopentanedione a potential biomarker for the consumption of this food product.
