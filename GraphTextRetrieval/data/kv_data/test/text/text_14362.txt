it is a 1,3-oxazole which is substituted by a [(2,4-dioxoimidazolidin-1-yl)imino]methyl group at position 2 and a 4-bromophenyl group at position 5. It is a muscle relaxant used for the treatment/prevention of malignant hyperthermia. It has a role as a ryanodine receptor modulator and a muscle relaxant. It is a member of 1,3-oxazoles, a member of bromobenzenes and an imidazolidine-2,4-dione. It is a conjugate acid of an it(1-).
