It has a role as a diuretic and an antihypertensive agent. it is a benzothiadiazide belonging to the class of thiazide diuretics. it is indicated as adjunctive therapy in edema and in the treatment of hypertension. In addition, this agent is capable of inhibiting rapid desensitization of the ionotropic alpha-amino-3-hydroxy-5-methylisoxazole-4-propionic acid (AMPA)-type glutamate receptors, thereby potentiating glutamate responses which may induce seizures activity. it was also found to inhibit gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA)-A receptors.
