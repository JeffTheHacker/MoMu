Tauro-beta-muricholic acid is a bile acid taurine conjugate derived from beta-muricholic acid. It has a role as a human metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a bile acid taurine conjugate, a monocarboxylic acid amide, a 3alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 6beta-hydroxy steroid and a 7beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a beta-muricholic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a tauro-beta-muricholate.
