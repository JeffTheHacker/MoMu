N,n&#39;-diphenyl-p-phenylenediamine appears as gray or dark gray powder or flakes. (NTP, 1992) it is an N-substituted diamine that is 1,4-phenylenediamine in which one hydrogen from each amino group is replaced by a phenyl group. It has a role as an antioxidant. It is a secondary amino compound and a N-substituted diamine. It derives from a p-aminodiphenylamine.
