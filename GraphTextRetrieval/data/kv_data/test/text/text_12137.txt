(1S,2S,5S)-2-(4-glutaridylbenzyl)-5-phenylcyclohexan-1-ol is a gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA)-based hapten where a 4-substituted benzoyl group is attached to nitrogen. It has a role as a hapten. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a member of cyclohexanols and a N-acyl-gamma-aminobutyric acid. It derives from a gamma-aminobutyric acid.
