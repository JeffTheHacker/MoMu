it is an aminoglycoside antibiotic that is (1S,3S)-3-ethyl-3,5,12-trihydroxy-10-methoxy-6,11-dioxo-1,2,3,4,6,11-hexahydrotetracene having a 3-amino-2,3,6-trideoxy-alpha-L-lyxo-hexopyranosyl residue attached at position 1 via a glycosidic linkage. It is an aminoglycoside antibiotic, an anthracycline, a deoxy hexoside, a monosaccharide derivative and a member of p-quinones. It is a conjugate acid of a it(1+). It derives from a hydride of a tetracene.
