2, 4, 6-Trihydroxybenzaldehyde, also known as phloroglucinol aldehyde, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as acylphloroglucinols and derivatives. These are phloroglucinol derivatives characterized by the presence of a COR group. 2, 4, 6-Trihydroxybenzaldehyde is slightly soluble (in water) and a very weakly acidic compound (based on its pKa).
