4-(3, 7-Dimethylocta-2, 6-dien-1-yl)-5-(6-hydroxy-1-benzofuran-2-yl)benzene-1, 3-diol belongs to the class of organic compounds known as 2-arylbenzofuran flavonoids. These are phenylpropanoids containing the 2-phenylbenzofuran moiety. 4-(3, 7-Dimethylocta-2, 6-dien-1-yl)-5-(6-hydroxy-1-benzofuran-2-yl)benzene-1, 3-diol is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule.
