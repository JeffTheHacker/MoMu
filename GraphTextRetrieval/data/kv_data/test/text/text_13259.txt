2-Propanoylthiophene, also known as ethyl 2-thienyl ketone or 1-(2-thienyl)propanone, belongs to the class of organic compounds known as aryl alkyl ketones. These are ketones have the generic structure RC(=O)R', where R = aryl group and R'=alkyl group. 2-Propanoylthiophene is considered to be a practically insoluble (in water) and relatively neutral molecule. Within the cell, 2-propanoylthiophene is primarily located in the cytoplasm. 2-Propanoylthiophene has a caramel and creamy taste.
