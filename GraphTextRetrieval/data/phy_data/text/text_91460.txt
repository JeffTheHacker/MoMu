Ecgonine is a tropane alkaloid that consists of tropane bearing carboxy and hydroxy substituents at positions 2 and 3 respectively and having (1R,2R,3S,5S)-configuration. It is both a metabolite of and a precursor to cocaine. It has a role as a metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a tropane alkaloid and a 2-hydroxy monocarboxylic acid.
