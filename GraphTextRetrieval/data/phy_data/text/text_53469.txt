Tecloftalam is a dicarboxylic acid monoamide that is the mono-(2,3-dichloroanilide) of tetrachlorophthalic acid. It is used for the treatment of bacterial leaf blight in paddy rice. It has a role as an antibacterial agent and an agrochemical. It is a dicarboxylic acid monoamide, a tetrachlorobenzene, a dichlorobenzene and a benzanilide fungicide.
