20,22-dihydrodigoxigenin is a hydroxy steroid that is the 20,22-dihydro derivative of digoxigenin, consisting of 5beta-cardanolide with hydroxy groups at the 3beta-, 12beta- and 14beta-positions. It is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a 12beta-hydroxy steroid and a 14beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a digoxigenin.
