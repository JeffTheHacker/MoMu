7alpha -hydroxytestosterone is an androstanoid that is testosterone substituted by a alpha-hydroxy group at position 7. A natural product found in Daphnia magna exposed to the biocide tributyltin It has a role as a Daphnia magna metabolite, a mouse metabolite and an androgen. It is a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a 7alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 17beta-hydroxy steroid and an androstanoid. It derives from a testosterone.
