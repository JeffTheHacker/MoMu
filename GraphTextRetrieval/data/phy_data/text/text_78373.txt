N,N-dimethylformamide dimethyl acetal is an acetal obtained by formal condensation of N,N-dimethylformamide with methanol. N,N-dimethylformamide dimethyl acetal is a derivatisation agent used in gas-chromatography applications It has a role as a chromatographic reagent. It is an acetal and a tertiary amino compound. It derives from a N,N-dimethylformamide.
