5'-xanthylic acid is a purine ribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate having xanthine as the nucleobase. It has a role as a metabolite, an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a purine ribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate and a xanthosine 5'-phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a 5'-xanthylate(2-).
