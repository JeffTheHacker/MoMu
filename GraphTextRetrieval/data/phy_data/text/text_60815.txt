Remifentanil is a piperidinecarboxylate ester that is methyl piperidine-4-carboxylate in which the hydrogen attached to the nitrogen is substituted by a 3-methoxy-3-oxopropyl group and the hydrogen at position 4 is substituted the nitrogen of N-propanoylaniline. It has a role as a mu-opioid receptor agonist, an opioid analgesic, an intravenous anaesthetic and a sedative. It is an anilide, a piperidinecarboxylate ester, a monocarboxylic acid amide and an alpha-amino acid ester.
