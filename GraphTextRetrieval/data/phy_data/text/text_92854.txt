3,17-dioxoandrost-4-en-19-al is an androstanoid that is androst-4-en-19-al substituted by oxo groups at positions 3 and 17. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 17-oxo steroid, a 19-oxo steroid, an androstanoid, a steroid aldehyde and a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid. It derives from an androst-4-ene-3,17-dione.
