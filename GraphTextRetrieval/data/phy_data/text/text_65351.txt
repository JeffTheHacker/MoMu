Pyrrolidine dithiocarbamate is a member of the class of dithiocarbamic acids that is the N-dithiocarboxy derivative of pyrrolidine. It has a role as an anticonvulsant, a neuroprotective agent, a radical scavenger, an antineoplastic agent, a NF-kappaB inhibitor and a geroprotector. It is a member of pyrrolidines and a member of dithiocarbamic acids.
