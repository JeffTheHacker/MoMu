S-(5-acetamido-2-hydroxyphenyl)-N-acetyl-L-cysteine is an S-substituted N-acetyl-L-cysteine in which the S-substitutuent is specified as 5-acetamido-2-hydroxyphenyl. It has a role as a drug metabolite, a human urinary metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a member of acetamides, an organic sulfide, a member of phenols and a S-substituted N-acetyl-L-cysteine. It derives from a paracetamol. It is a conjugate acid of a S-(5-acetamido-2-hydroxyphenyl)-N-acetyl-L-cysteinate.
