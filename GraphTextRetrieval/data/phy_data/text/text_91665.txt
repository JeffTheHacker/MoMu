Pyroquilon is a pyrroloquinoline that is 1,2,5,6-tetrahydro-4H-pyrrolo[3,2,1-ij]quinoline in which the hydrogens at position 4 are replaced by an oxo group. A fungicide used to control rice blast, it is not approved wof use within the European Union. It has a role as a melanin synthesis inhibitor and an antifungal agrochemical. It is a pyrroloquinoline and a delta-lactam.
