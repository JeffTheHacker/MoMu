5-(methylamino)-2-phenyl-4-[3-(trifluoromethyl)phenyl]furan-3(2H)-one is a member of the class of furans that is furan-3(2H)-one which is substituted at positions 2, 4, and 5 by phenyl, m-(trifluoromethyl)phenyl, and methylamino groups, respectively. It is a member of furans, a cyclic ketone, a secondary amino compound and a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes.
