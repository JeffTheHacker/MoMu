5-pyridoxolactone is a furopyridine that is furo[3,4-c]pyridin-3(1H)-one substituted by a hydroxy group at position 7 and a methoxy group at position 6. It is a metabolite of vitamin B6. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and a human urinary metabolite. It is a furopyridine, a lactone and a member of phenols. It derives from a 5-pyridoxic acid.
