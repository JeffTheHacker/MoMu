3,7-diamino-5-phenylphenazin-5-ium is an organic cation that is 5-phenylphenothiazin-5-ium substituted by amino groups at positions 3 and 7 as well as a nitro group at position 4. The chloride salt is the histological dye 'safranin O'. It has a role as a histological dye and a fluorochrome. It is an organic cation and a member of phenazines.
