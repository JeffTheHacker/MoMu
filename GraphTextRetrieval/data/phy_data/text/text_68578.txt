4-methoxy-17beta-estradiol is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid that is 17beta-estradiol in which the hydrogen at position 4 has been replaced by a methoxy group. It has a role as an estrogen, a human metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, a 3-hydroxy steroid, an aromatic ether and a member of phenols. It derives from a 17beta-estradiol.
