Methylprednisolone 21-suleptanic acid ester is a carboxylic ester resulting from the formal condensation of the 21-hydroxy group of 6alpha-methylprednisolone with the carboxy group of 8-[methyl(2-sulfoethyl)amino]-8-oxooctanoic acid. It is a carboxylic ester, an 11beta-hydroxy steroid, a 17alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 20-oxo steroid, a 3-oxo-Delta(1),Delta(4)-steroid, a tertiary carboxamide and an organosulfonic acid. It derives from a 6alpha-methylprednisolone. It is a conjugate acid of a methylprednisolone 21-suleptanic acid ester(1-).
